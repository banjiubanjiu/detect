# Russian forces launch nearly 60 attacks on Dnipro region, four killed and 24 wounded

This was reported by the head of the regional military administration, Oleksandr Hanzha, on Telegram, according to Ukrinform.

According to him, in the Nikopol district, the communities of Nikopol, Marhanets, Chervonohryhorivka, Pokrovsk, and Myrove came under attack.

A sports school, vehicles, apartment buildings, and private homes were damaged.

In Nikopol, four people were killed as a result of an attack on a bus. They were women aged 57, 73, and 85, and a 53-year-old man.

In the region, 24 people were injured in Russian strikes, eight of whom were hospitalized. The rest are receiving outpatient treatment, Hanzha noted.

In the Kryvyi Rih district, Russian forces struck the Zelenodolsk community. A mobile crane and a private home were damaged.

In the Pavlohrad district, the invaders targeted the Yurivka community. Infrastructure was damaged.

As reported by Ukrinform, Russian invaders attacked a passenger bus in the Nikopol district for the second time that day. Five people were injured.
