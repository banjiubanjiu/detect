# Qatar says four wounded, including a child, by missile debris

7 April 2026 23:25 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/iran%20university%20bomb%20reuters.jpg.jpg?itok=YPsB7zM9)

Qatar's interior ministry said on Wednesday that falling debris from an Iranian missile attack had hurt four people, including a child, as Iran presses an aerial campaign against Gulf states.

"Security authorities have begun dealing with an incident resulting from the interception by Qatari air defences of Iranian missiles, which led to debris falling on the home of a citizen in the Muriykh area," the ministry said in a statement, referring to a residential area in the west of Doha.

"The incident resulted in the recording of four moderate injuries, including a Qatari child," the statement added.
