# Survivors of Iran bridge attack recall terror of US-Israeli strike

People who survived an Israeli-US attack on Iran’s biggest road bridge have spoken of their terror as missiles hit the structure. Iran has condemned strikes on civilian infrastructure, saying it’s a sign of “moral collapse” on the part of its enemies.
