# As I see itWhy Donald Trump now needs a ‘forever war’

The US president and America are starting to understand you can’t go fascist at home and imperialist abroad halfway

![US President Donald Trump prepares to speak about the war on Iran at the White House in Washington on April 1. Photo: AP](https://cdn.i-scmp.com/sites/default/files/styles/1020x680/public/d8/images/canvas/2026/04/07/3d9358fa-82cb-4056-857d-31036747b39b_694cfa63.jpg?itok=dsjIxp3J&v=1775533850)

It might have made a good April Fool’s joke. Unfortunately, it was not.

“Tuesday will be Power Plant Day, and Bridge Day, all wrapped up in one, in Iran. There will be nothing like it!!! Open the F***in’ Strait, you crazy b*****ds, or you’ll be living in Hell – JUST WATCH! Praise be to Allah. President DONALD J. TRUMP.”

Really, who writes like that? Even a tinpot dictator from some godforsaken hellhole would have written a less expletive-laden post to retain some personal dignity as a leader of sorts. We are, unfortunately, quoting the president of a country long identified as the leader of the “Free World”.

If all that were to happen, Trump and his cronies would finally face the music, with endless lawsuits, investigations and possibly even prison once he leaves office.
