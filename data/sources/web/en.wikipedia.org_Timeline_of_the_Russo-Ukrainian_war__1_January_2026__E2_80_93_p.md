January 2026
------------

### 1 January

The Russian-installed governor of [Kherson Oblast](/wiki/Kherson_Oblast "Kherson Oblast"), [Vladimir Saldo](/wiki/Vladimir_Saldo "Vladimir Saldo"), said 28 people were killed while several others were injured in a Ukrainian drone strike on hotels and restaurants in [Khorly](/w/index.php?title=Khorly&action=edit&redlink=1 "Khorly (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A5%D0%BE%D1%80%D0%BB%D0%B8 "uk:Хорли")]. Saldo accused Ukraine of deliberately targeting civilians during [New Year's Eve](/wiki/New_Year%27s_Eve "New Year's Eve") celebrations.[[1]](#cite_note-1)

Multiple animals were killed and injured in a Russian airstrike on the [Feldman Ecopark](/wiki/Feldman_Ecopark "Feldman Ecopark") in [Lisne](/w/index.php?title=Lisne,_Kharkiv_Raion&action=edit&redlink=1 "Lisne, Kharkiv Raion (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%9B%D1%96%D1%81%D0%BD%D0%B5_(%D0%A5%D0%B0%D1%80%D0%BA%D1%96%D0%B2%D1%81%D1%8C%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9_%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B9%D0%BE%D0%BD) "uk:Лісне (Харківський район)")], [Kharkiv Oblast](/wiki/Kharkiv_Oblast "Kharkiv Oblast").[[2]](#cite_note-2)

Drones were reported over [Moscow](/wiki/Moscow "Moscow"), shutting down local airports. They were believed to be timed to interfere with President [Putin](/wiki/Vladimir_Putin "Vladimir Putin")'s New Year's speech.[[3]](#cite_note-3) Ukrainian drones struck the [Ilsky](/wiki/Ilsky "Ilsky") oil refinery in [Krasnodar Krai](/wiki/Krasnodar_Krai "Krasnodar Krai") and various sites in occupied [Donetsk Oblast](/wiki/Donetsk_Oblast "Donetsk Oblast").[[4]](#cite_note-4)

[Germany](/wiki/Germany "Germany") delivered two [Patriot missile systems](/wiki/Patriot_missile_systems "Patriot missile systems") to Ukraine.[[5]](#cite_note-5)

### 2 January

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ef/Destructions_in_Kharkiv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-01-02_%2801%29.jpg/250px-Destructions_in_Kharkiv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-01-02_%2801%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:Destructions_in_Kharkiv_after_Russian_attack,_2026-01-02_(01).jpg)

An apartment building in Kharkiv after the attack

Six people were killed in a Russian missile attack on [Kharkiv](/wiki/Kharkiv "Kharkiv").[[6]](#cite_note-6)

[Kyrylo Budanov](/wiki/Kyrylo_Budanov "Kyrylo Budanov"), head of the [HUR](/wiki/Main_Directorate_of_Intelligence_(Ukraine) "Main Directorate of Intelligence (Ukraine)"), was appointed to replace [Andriy Yermak](/wiki/Andriy_Yermak "Andriy Yermak") as head of the [Office of the President of Ukraine](/wiki/Office_of_the_President_of_Ukraine "Office of the President of Ukraine").[[7]](#cite_note-7)

Ukrainian drones struck the [Novokuybyshevsk](/wiki/Novokuybyshevsk "Novokuybyshevsk") Oil Refinery in [Samara Oblast](/wiki/Samara_Oblast "Samara Oblast").[[8]](#cite_note-8)

Geolocated footage showed Ukrainian forces operating in [Drobysheve](/wiki/Drobysheve "Drobysheve") near [Lyman](/wiki/Lyman,_Ukraine "Lyman, Ukraine"), an area Russian forces claimed they controlled. Geolocated footage showed Russian advances in the city of [Kostiantynivka](/wiki/Kostiantynivka "Kostiantynivka") and northwest of the village of [Oleksandro-Kalynove](/wiki/Oleksandro-Kalynove "Oleksandro-Kalynove"); and Russian advances towards [Hryhorivka](/w/index.php?title=Hryhorivka,_Zaporizhzhia_Oblast&action=edit&redlink=1 "Hryhorivka, Zaporizhzhia Oblast (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%93%D1%80%D0%B8%D0%B3%D0%BE%D1%80%D1%96%D0%B2%D0%BA%D0%B0_(%D0%97%D0%B0%D0%BF%D0%BE%D1%80%D1%96%D0%B7%D1%8C%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9_%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B9%D0%BE%D0%BD) "uk:Григорівка (Запорізький район)")] and [Prymorske](/wiki/Prymorske,_Zaporizhzhia_Oblast "Prymorske, Zaporizhzhia Oblast") near the [Konka river](/wiki/Konka_(Zaporizhzhia_Oblast) "Konka (Zaporizhzhia Oblast)").[[9]](#cite_note-9)

### 3 January

In [Dnipropetrovsk](/wiki/Dnipropetrovsk_Oblast "Dnipropetrovsk Oblast") and [Zaporizhzhia Oblasts](/wiki/Zaporizhzhia_Oblast "Zaporizhzhia Oblast"), 44 settlements were subjected to mandatory evacuation due to the deteriorating "security situation", affecting some 3,000 children and families.[[10]](#cite_note-10)

Geolocated footage indicated Russian advances in central [Sotnytskyi Kozachok](/wiki/Sotnytskyi_Kozachok "Sotnytskyi Kozachok"), northwest of Kharkiv; and northeast of [Orikhovo-Vasylivka](/wiki/Orikhovo-Vasylivka "Orikhovo-Vasylivka") near the [E40 highway](/wiki/European_route_E40_in_Ukraine "European route E40 in Ukraine").[[11]](#cite_note-11)

### 4 January

The Russian Defense Ministry claimed Russian forces seized [Podoly](/wiki/Podoly "Podoly") near [Kupiansk](/wiki/Kupiansk "Kupiansk"). The [Head of Chechnya](/wiki/Head_of_the_Chechen_Republic "Head of the Chechen Republic"), [Ramzan Kadyrov](/wiki/Ramzan_Kadyrov "Ramzan Kadyrov"), claimed the Chechen [270th Akhmat-Kavkaz Motorized Rifle Regiment](/w/index.php?title=270th_Akhmat-Kavkaz_Motorized_Rifle_Regiment&action=edit&redlink=1 "270th Akhmat-Kavkaz Motorized Rifle Regiment (page does not exist)") took the village of [Bilohiria](/w/index.php?title=Bilohiria,_Zaporizhzhia_Oblast&action=edit&redlink=1 "Bilohiria, Zaporizhzhia Oblast (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%91%D1%96%D0%BB%D0%BE%D0%B3%D1%96%D1%80%27%D1%8F_(%D1%81%D0%B5%D0%BB%D0%BE) "uk:Білогір'я (село)")], southeast of [Orikhiv](/wiki/Orikhiv "Orikhiv").[[12]](#cite_note-12)

Russian authorities said two people were killed in Ukrainian drone strikes on [Belgorod](/wiki/Belgorod_Oblast "Belgorod Oblast") and [Kursk Oblasts](/wiki/Kursk_Oblast "Kursk Oblast").[[13]](#cite_note-13)

Two people, including a [National Guard of Ukraine](/wiki/National_Guard_of_Ukraine "National Guard of Ukraine") officer, were injured in a car bombing in [Obolonskyi District](/wiki/Obolonskyi_District "Obolonskyi District") in Kyiv. A suspect was arrested.[[14]](#cite_note-14)

The [mayor of Moscow](/wiki/Mayor_of_Moscow "Mayor of Moscow") claimed around 28 drones approached the city, leading to the cancellation of more than 200 flights as the [Vnukovo](/wiki/Vnukovo_International_Airport "Vnukovo International Airport"), [Domodedovo](/wiki/Moscow_Domodedovo_Airport "Moscow Domodedovo Airport"), and [Zhukovsky](/wiki/Zhukovsky_International_Airport "Zhukovsky International Airport") airports were closed.[[15]](#cite_note-15) Ukrainian drones struck the Energia enterprise at [Yelets](/wiki/Yelets "Yelets") in [Lipetsk Oblast](/wiki/Lipetsk_Oblast "Lipetsk Oblast"). The plant makes batteries for various Russian weapon systems.[[16]](#cite_note-16)

President [Zelenskyy](/wiki/Volodymyr_Zelenskyy "Volodymyr Zelenskyy") dismissed Lieutenant General [Serhii Deineko](/wiki/Serhii_Deineko "Serhii Deineko") as head of the [State Border Guard Service of Ukraine](/wiki/State_Border_Guard_Service_of_Ukraine "State Border Guard Service of Ukraine"), and replaced him on an interim basis with his deputy [Valeriy Vavrynyuk](/wiki/Valeriy_Vavrynyuk "Valeriy Vavrynyuk").[[17]](#cite_note-17)

### 5 January

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/20/Destructions_in_Kyiv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-01-05_%2801%29.jpg/250px-Destructions_in_Kyiv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-01-05_%2801%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:Destructions_in_Kyiv_after_Russian_attack,_2026-01-05_(01).jpg)

Clinic in Kyiv after the attack

[Vasyl Malyuk](/wiki/Vasyl_Malyuk "Vasyl Malyuk") was forced to resign as head of the [SBU](/wiki/Security_Service_of_Ukraine "Security Service of Ukraine") by President Zelenskyy.[[18]](#cite_note-18)

One person was killed in a Russian drone attack on Kyiv.[[19]](#cite_note-19)

Russia claimed to have taken [Hrabovske](/wiki/Hrabovske "Hrabovske") in Sumy Oblast.[[20]](#cite_note-20)

Ukrainian drones destroyed a Russian [9S32M1 radar](/wiki/9S32 "9S32") of an [S-300V](/wiki/S-300V "S-300V") missile system in Donetsk Oblast.[[21]](#cite_note-21)

The Russian [136th Separate Guards Motor Rifle Brigade](/wiki/136th_Separate_Guards_Motor_Rifle_Brigade "136th Separate Guards Motor Rifle Brigade")’s commander, Colonel [Eric Selimov](/w/index.php?title=Eric_Selimov&action=edit&redlink=1 "Eric Selimov (page does not exist)"), was killed in a car crash in [Alchevsk Raion](/wiki/Alchevsk_Raion "Alchevsk Raion") in [Luhansk Oblast](/wiki/Luhansk_Oblast "Luhansk Oblast").[[22]](#cite_note-22)

Ukrainian military observer [Kostyantyn Mashovets](/w/index.php?title=Kostyantyn_Mashovets&action=edit&redlink=1 "Kostyantyn Mashovets (page does not exist)") reported Russian advances north of Yablunivka across [Highway H20](/wiki/Highway_H20_(Ukraine) "Highway H20 (Ukraine)"), and also in southeast Kostiantynivka.[[23]](#cite_note-23)

### 6 January

A Russian milblogger claimed Russian forces took the village of [Pishchane](/wiki/Pishchane,_Kharkiv_Oblast "Pishchane, Kharkiv Oblast"), southeast of Kupiansk.[[24]](#cite_note-russian-offensive-campaign-assessment-january-6-2026-24)

Ukrainian drones struck targets in [Penza](/wiki/Penza "Penza") and [Sterlitamak](/wiki/Sterlitamak "Sterlitamak").[[25]](#cite_note-25) Ukrainian drones struck [Yaroslavl](/wiki/Yaroslavl "Yaroslavl"), with Yaroslavl Oil Refinery believed to be the target.[[26]](#cite_note-26) Ukrainian forces attacked the 100th Arsenal of the [Main Missile and Artillery Directorate](/wiki/Main_Missile_and_Artillery_Directorate "Main Missile and Artillery Directorate") located in [Neya](/wiki/Neya_(town) "Neya (town)") in [Kostroma Oblast](/wiki/Kostroma_Oblast "Kostroma Oblast"), which supplied ammunitions storages in the [Central](/wiki/Central_Military_District "Central Military District") and [Moscow](/wiki/Moscow_Military_District "Moscow Military District") military districts. Ukrainian forces also attacked an oil depot in [Usmansky District](/wiki/Usmansky_District "Usmansky District") in [Lipetsk Oblast](/wiki/Lipetsk_Oblast "Lipetsk Oblast").[[24]](#cite_note-russian-offensive-campaign-assessment-january-6-2026-24)

### 7 January

Ukrainian drones set fire to the [Oskolneftesnab oil depot](/w/index.php?title=Oskolneftesnab_oil_depot&action=edit&redlink=1 "Oskolneftesnab oil depot (page does not exist)") near [Kotel](/w/index.php?title=Kotel,_Belgorod_Oblast&action=edit&redlink=1 "Kotel, Belgorod Oblast (page does not exist)") in Belgorod Oblast. A warehouse belonging to the Russian [20th Motor Rifle Division](/w/index.php?title=20th_Motor_Rifle_Division&action=edit&redlink=1 "20th Motor Rifle Division (page does not exist)") in [Donetsk](/wiki/Donetsk "Donetsk") was also struck.[[27]](#cite_note-27)

Russian forces struck [Chornomorsk](/wiki/Port_of_Chornomorsk "Port of Chornomorsk") and [Pivdennyi](/wiki/Pivdennyi_Port "Pivdennyi Port") ports in [Odesa Oblast](/wiki/Odesa_Oblast "Odesa Oblast"), killing one person and injuring five more.[[28]](#cite_note-28)

Geolocated footage showed Russian advances in north [Yunakivka](/wiki/Yunakivka "Yunakivka") and Ukrainian advances in [Vovchanski Khutory](/wiki/Vovchanski_Khutory "Vovchanski Khutory").[[29]](#cite_note-29)

### 8 January

Russian forces struck [Lviv](/wiki/Lviv "Lviv") with an [Oreshnik](/wiki/Oreshnik_(missile) "Oreshnik (missile)") missile.[[30]](#cite_note-russian-offensive-campaign-assessment-january-9-2026-30)

DeepstateUA reported Russian forces took the villages of [Andriivka](/wiki/Andriivka,_Sumy_Raion "Andriivka, Sumy Raion") in Sumy Oblast and [Novomarkove](/w/index.php?title=Novomarkove&action=edit&redlink=1 "Novomarkove (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%9D%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%BE%D0%BC%D0%B0%D1%80%D0%BA%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%B5 "uk:Новомаркове")] in Donetsk Oblast.[[31]](#cite_note-31) The Ukrainian [Kursk Group of Forces](/w/index.php?title=Kursk_Group_of_Forces&action=edit&redlink=1 "Kursk Group of Forces (page does not exist)") denied the claim of Andriivka being captured.[[32]](#cite_note-32)

A [Russian shadow fleet](/wiki/Russian_shadow_fleet "Russian shadow fleet") oil tanker *Elbus* was struck by a drone off the coast of Turkey. According to ASTRA, the tanker was headed to [Novorossiysk](/wiki/Novorossiysk "Novorossiysk") and sustained damage, but no crew members were injured. The ship was towed to [İnebolu](/wiki/%C4%B0nebolu "İnebolu") for inspections.[[33]](#cite_note-33)

The pro-Ukrainian [Freedom of Russia Legion](/wiki/Freedom_of_Russia_Legion "Freedom of Russia Legion") set fire to some 15 trucks at the village of [Kochegury](/wiki/Kochegury "Kochegury") in Belgorod Oblast.[[34]](#cite_note-34)

Geolocated footage showed Ukrainian forces continued to operate in [Zahryzove](/w/index.php?title=Zahryzove&action=edit&redlink=1 "Zahryzove (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%97%D0%B0%D0%B3%D1%80%D0%B8%D0%B7%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%B5 "uk:Загризове")] near [Borova](/wiki/Borova,_Izium_Raion,_Kharkiv_Oblast "Borova, Izium Raion, Kharkiv Oblast"), an area Russian forces claimed they controlled, while the Russian Defense Ministry claimed Russian forces took the village of [Bratske](/w/index.php?title=Bratske,_Synelnykove_Raion,_Dnipropetrovsk_Oblast&action=edit&redlink=1 "Bratske, Synelnykove Raion, Dnipropetrovsk Oblast (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%91%D1%80%D0%B0%D1%82%D1%81%D1%8C%D0%BA%D0%B5_(%D0%A1%D0%B8%D0%BD%D0%B5%D0%BB%D1%8C%D0%BD%D0%B8%D0%BA%D1%96%D0%B2%D1%81%D1%8C%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9_%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B9%D0%BE%D0%BD) "uk:Братське (Синельниківський район)")], south of [Pokrovske](/wiki/Pokrovske,_Synelnykove_Raion,_Dnipropetrovsk_Oblast "Pokrovske, Synelnykove Raion, Dnipropetrovsk Oblast").[[35]](#cite_note-35)

### 9 January

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ec/Destructions_in_Kyiv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-01-09_%2801%29.jpg/250px-Destructions_in_Kyiv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-01-09_%2801%29.jpg)](/wiki/File:Destructions_in_Kyiv_after_Russian_attack,_2026-01-09_(01).jpg)

Apartment building in Kyiv after the attack

Four people were killed in Russian airstrikes on Kyiv that also damaged the building housing the Qatari embassy.[[36]](#cite_note-36)

Ukrainian [FP-2 drones](/wiki/Ukrspecsystems_PD-2 "Ukrspecsystems PD-2") struck a fuel train in Crimea and a military arms depot in Donetsk.[[37]](#cite_note-37) Ukrainian missiles struck the thermal power plants at [Belgorod](/wiki/Belgorod "Belgorod") and [Oryol](/wiki/Oryol "Oryol"), resulting in blackouts.[[38]](#cite_note-38)

The Minister for Development of Communities and Territories [Oleksiy Kuleba](/wiki/Oleksiy_Kuleba "Oleksiy Kuleba"), said Russian forces struck a vessel en route to [Chornomorsk](/wiki/Chornomorsk "Chornomorsk"), and another vessel near Odesa, killing a [Syrian](/wiki/Syrians "Syrians") national.[[39]](#cite_note-39)

Russian milbloggers claimed Russian forces took the village of [Tyshchenkivka](/w/index.php?title=Tyshchenkivka&action=edit&redlink=1 "Tyshchenkivka (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A2%D0%B8%D1%89%D0%B5%D0%BD%D0%BA%D1%96%D0%B2%D0%BA%D0%B0 "uk:Тищенківка")], northwest of Kupiansk. Geolocated footage showed Russian advances in west [Novomarkove](/w/index.php?title=Novomarkove&action=edit&redlink=1 "Novomarkove (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%9D%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%BE%D0%BC%D0%B0%D1%80%D0%BA%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%B5 "uk:Новомаркове")], and northwest of [Stupochky](/w/index.php?title=Stupochky&action=edit&redlink=1 "Stupochky (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%A1%D1%82%D1%83%D0%BF%D0%BE%D1%87%D0%BA%D0%B8 "uk:Ступочки")] and [Ivanopillia](/w/index.php?title=Ivanopillia&action=edit&redlink=1 "Ivanopillia (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%86%D0%B2%D0%B0%D0%BD%D0%BE%D0%BF%D1%96%D0%BB%D0%BB%D1%8F "uk:Іванопілля")], all located east of Kostiantynivka. Geolocated footage showed Ukrainian advances west of [Pryluky](/w/index.php?title=Pryluky,_Polohy_Raion&action=edit&redlink=1 "Pryluky, Polohy Raion (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%9F%D1%80%D0%B8%D0%BB%D1%83%D0%BA%D0%B8_(%D0%9F%D0%BE%D0%BB%D0%BE%D0%B3%D1%96%D0%B2%D1%81%D1%8C%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9_%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B9%D0%BE%D0%BD) "uk:Прилуки (Пологівський район)")] and Russian infiltration in [Zelene](/w/index.php?title=Zelene,_Polohy_Raion&action=edit&redlink=1 "Zelene, Polohy Raion (page does not exist)") [[uk](https://uk.wikipedia.org/wiki/%D0%97%D0%B5%D0%BB%D0%B5%D0%BD%D0%B5_(%D0%9F%D0%BE%D0%BB%D0%BE%D0%B3%D1%96%D0%B2%D1%81%D1%8C%D0%BA%D0%B8%D0%B9_%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B9%D0%BE%D0%BD) "uk:Зелене (Пологівський район)")], both located north of [Huliaipole](/wiki/Huliaipole "Huliaipole"). Geolocated footage showed Ukrainian forces continued to operate in central [Stepnohirsk](/wiki/Stepnohirsk "Stepnohirsk"), an area Russian forces claimed they controlled.[[30]](#cite_note-russian-offensive-campaign-assessment-january-9-2026-30)

The SBU arrested two [GRU](/wiki/GRU_(Russian_Federation) "GRU (Russian Federation)") agents who were accused of assessing the effectiveness of the [Oreshnik missile](/wiki/Oreshnik_missile "Oreshnik missile") strike on Lviv.[[40]](#cite_note-40)

### 10 January

### 24 January

According to the regional governor [Vyacheslav Gladkov](/wiki/Vyacheslav_Gladkov "Vyacheslav Gladkov"), Belgorod Oblast suffered its "most massive" attack that involved that HIMARS, damaging electrical infrastructure, a utility building, a car park and homes in the village of [Tavrovo](/wiki/Tavrovo "Tavrovo").[[91]](#cite_note-91) Unidentified drones also struck [Taganrog](/wiki/Taganrog "Taganrog") in Rostov Oblast, with the city losing power after explosions and fires were reported by locals with a transformer failing. Explosions were also reported in [Oryol](/wiki/Oryol "Oryol") and [Bryansk Oblast](/wiki/Bryansk_Oblast "Bryansk Oblast").[[92]](#cite_note-92)

A Russian drone attack damaged two buildings of the [Kyiv Pechersk Lavra](/wiki/Kyiv_Pechersk_Lavra "Kyiv Pechersk Lavra"), the first time the complex sustained war-related damage since [World War II](/wiki/World_War_II "World War II").[[93]](#cite_note-93)

### 26 January

An oil refinery in [Slavyansk-on-Kuban](/wiki/Slavyansk-on-Kuban "Slavyansk-on-Kuban") was attacked by Ukrainian drones with at least one oil storage tank being set on fire. The [Novokuybyshevsk](/wiki/Novokuybyshevsk "Novokuybyshevsk") Oil Refinery in Samara Oblast was also attacked.[[94]](#cite_note-94)

### 27 January

### 28 January

Ukrainian forces struck the [Khokholskaya](/wiki/Khokholsky_District "Khokholsky District") oil depot in [Voronezh Oblast](/wiki/Voronezh_Oblast "Voronezh Oblast") with thick smoke being spotted over the depot. Ukrainian forces struck Russian soldiers in [Kolotilovka](/wiki/Kolotilovka "Kolotilovka"), [Belgorod Oblast](/wiki/Belgorod_Oblast "Belgorod Oblast"). Other groupings of Russian soldiers were attacked in [Velyka Novosilka](/wiki/Velyka_Novosilka "Velyka Novosilka"), [Shakhove](/w/index.php?title=Shakhove&action=edit&redlink=1 "Shakhove (page does not exist)"), and [Hryhoriivka](/w/index.php?title=Hryhoriivka&action=edit&redlink=1 "Hryhoriivka (page does not exist)"), [Donetsk Oblast](/wiki/Donetsk_Oblast "Donetsk Oblast"). A UAV control center near [Velyka Novosilka](/wiki/Velyka_Novosilka "Velyka Novosilka") and battalion command post near [Berezove](/wiki/Berezove,_Synelnykove_Raion,_Dnipropetrovsk_Oblast "Berezove, Synelnykove Raion, Dnipropetrovsk Oblast"), both in Russian-occupied [Dnipropetrovsk Oblast](/wiki/Dnipropetrovsk_Oblast "Dnipropetrovsk Oblast") was attacked as was an ammunition depot in [Nyzhnia Duvanka](/wiki/Nyzhnia_Duvanka "Nyzhnia Duvanka"), Russian-occupied [Luhansk Oblast](/wiki/Luhansk_Oblast "Luhansk Oblast").[[100]](#cite_note-100)

Ukrainian officials claimed that Russian forces lost a [Su-34](/wiki/Su-34 "Su-34") and a [Su-30](/wiki/Su-30 "Su-30"), without providing further details. On social media a Su-34 was reported destroyed along with its crew.[[101]](#cite_note-101)

According to Komarenko, in total Ukrainian forces had cleared 400 km2 (150 sq mi) of territory since the beginning of counteroffensive operations, and February 2026 became the first month since 2024 when Ukraine regained more territory than it lost.[[250]](#cite_note-250)

Ukraine launched a drone attack on Sevastopol, damaging a building belonging to the [3rd Radio-Technical Air Defense Regiment](/wiki/Russian_Radio-Technical_Troops "Russian Radio-Technical Troops"). Governor [Mikhail Razvozhayev](/wiki/Mikhail_Razvozhayev "Mikhail Razvozhayev") claimed that one person had died and two were wounded. An apartment building caught fire. The Ukrainian military confirmed it had carried out strikes on equipment supporting electronic warfare and radio reconnaissance systems in Sevastopol and a logistics warehouse at [Khersones air base](/wiki/Khersones_air_base "Khersones air base").[[292]](#cite_note-292)

In [Stavropol Krai](/wiki/Stavropol_Krai "Stavropol Krai"), [Nevinnomyssk](/wiki/Nevinnomyssk "Nevinnomyssk") was struck by drones, with locals reporting explosions. The governor confirmed the city's “industrial zone” was attacked, with the Nevinnomyssk Azot chemical plant the likely target.[[293]](#cite_note-293)

A court in Kyiv suspended Yaroslav Lysenko, commander of the 43rd Brigade, from his position on charges of abuse of power and helping military personnel evade service by employing them on personal businesses in Kyiv and [Cherkasy Oblasts](/wiki/Cherkasy_Oblast "Cherkasy Oblast").[[294]](#cite_note-294)

### 20 March

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/22/Russian_missile_Iskander%2C_found_in_Chernihiv_Oblast%2C_2026-03-20_%2805%29.webp/250px-Russian_missile_Iskander%2C_found_in_Chernihiv_Oblast%2C_2026-03-20_%2805%29.webp.png)](/wiki/File:Russian_missile_Iskander,_found_in_Chernihiv_Oblast,_2026-03-20_(05).webp)

Removal of an [Iskander](/wiki/9K720_Iskander "9K720 Iskander") missile found on a field in Chernihiv Oblast

The governor of Belgorod Oblast claimed that one person was killed in a Ukrainian drone strike on [Murom](/wiki/Murom,_Belgorod_Oblast "Murom, Belgorod Oblast").[[295]](#cite_note-295)

Ukrainian drones struck the [Alchevsk](/wiki/Alchevsk "Alchevsk") Metallurgical Plant in Luhansk Oblast. Four explosions were reported; previously, the Ukrainian General Staff said the plant was making shell casings.[[296]](#cite_note-296) A Ukrainian fibre optic drone shot down a [Ka-52](/wiki/Ka-52 "Ka-52") helicopter “in the Pokrovsk sector”, according to an aide to the Ukrainian Defence Minister. The crew ejected, but were later killed by another drone.[[297]](#cite_note-297) [[298]](#cite_note-298) A second Ka-52 was reported shot down by a Russian milblogger in “unclear” circumstances.[[299]](#cite_note-299)

Russian air defences near Moscow shot down a light aircraft, killing both pilots, having mistaken it for a Ukrainian drone during an attack on the city.[[300]](#cite_note-300)

### 21 March

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/7c/Building_of_former_Bank_of_Mutual_Crediting_Society_in_Kherson_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-03-21_%2802%29.webp/250px-Building_of_former_Bank_of_Mutual_Crediting_Society_in_Kherson_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-03-21_%2802%29.webp.png)](/wiki/File:Building_of_former_Bank_of_Mutual_Crediting_Society_in_Kherson_after_Russian_attack,_2026-03-21_(02).webp)

Historical bank building (1899) in Kherson after drone attack on 21 March

Two people were killed in a Russian attack on Zaporizhzhia.[[301]](#cite_note-301) One person was killed in a separate attack in [Maidan](/w/index.php?title=Maidan,_Donetsk_Oblast&action=edit&redlink=1 "Maidan, Donetsk Oblast (page does not exist)"), Donetsk Oblast.[[302]](#cite_note-302)

Ukrainian drones struck targets in [Tolyatti](/wiki/Tolyatti "Tolyatti") in [Samara Oblast](/wiki/Samara_Oblast "Samara Oblast") and [Saratov](/wiki/Saratov "Saratov") and [Engels](/wiki/Engels,_Saratov_Oblast "Engels, Saratov Oblast") in [Saratov Oblast](/wiki/Saratov_Oblast "Saratov Oblast"). Saratov governor [Roman Busargin](/wiki/Roman_Busargin "Roman Busargin") claimed two people were injured and houses were damaged. Locals reported power outages and an attack on the oil refinery.[[303]](#cite_note-303) The Ukrainian military also struck a command post for the Rubicon drone unit in [Mariupol](/wiki/Mariupol "Mariupol").[[304]](#cite_note-304)

Geolocated footage showed that Russian forces took the town of [Rodynske](/wiki/Rodynske "Rodynske") on a prior date.[[305]](#cite_note-305)

### 22 March

Ukrainian drones struck an industrial zone close to the oil refinery in [Ufa](/wiki/Ufa "Ufa"). A plume of smoke, gunfire and explosions were reported by locals.[[306]](#cite_note-306)

### 23 March

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-23_Primorsk.png/250px-NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-23_Primorsk.png)](/wiki/File:NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-23_Primorsk.png)

[NASA](/wiki/NASA "NASA")'s [FIRMS](/wiki/Fire_Information_for_Resource_Management_System "Fire Information for Resource Management System") detected extensive fires on 23 March 2026 00:45:00 ([UTC](/wiki/UTC "UTC")) at the port of [Primorsk, Leningrad Oblast](/wiki/Primorsk,_Leningrad_Oblast "Primorsk, Leningrad Oblast")

The port facilities at [Primorsk, Leningrad Oblast](/wiki/Primorsk,_Leningrad_Oblast "Primorsk, Leningrad Oblast") were damaged after a drone attack set a fuel tank on fire. [Pulkovo Airport](/wiki/Pulkovo_Airport "Pulkovo Airport") was temporarily closed.[[307]](#cite_note-307)

Ukrainian [Unmanned Systems Forces](/wiki/Unmanned_Systems_Forces_(Ukraine) "Unmanned Systems Forces (Ukraine)") struck a "Shahed-type UAV" depot in Donetsk Oblast using [Fire Point](/wiki/Fire_Point_(Ukrainian_firm) "Fire Point (Ukrainian firm)") drones. A similar attack was carried out on a train in [Luhansk Oblast](/wiki/Luhansk_Oblast "Luhansk Oblast").[[308]](#cite_note-308) A strike was conducted on a workshop in [Avdiivka](/wiki/Avdiivka "Avdiivka") that Ukraine claimed was “used to manufacture chemical munitions” such as [chloropicrin](/wiki/Chloropicrin "Chloropicrin").[[309]](#cite_note-309)

### 24 March

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d1/Destructions_in_the_architectural_complex_of_Bernardine_monastery_in_Lviv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-03-24_%28000%29.webm/250px-seek%3D3-Destructions_in_the_architectural_complex_of_Bernardine_monastery_in_Lviv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-03-24_%28000%29.webm.jpg)](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/transcoded/d/d1/Destructions_in_the_architectural_complex_of_Bernardine_monastery_in_Lviv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-03-24_%28000%29.webm/Destructions_in_the_architectural_complex_of_Bernardine_monastery_in_Lviv_after_Russian_attack%2C_2026-03-24_%28000%29.webm.480p.vp9.webm)

Architectural complex of [Bernardine monastery](/wiki/Bernardine_Church,_Lviv "Bernardine Church, Lviv") in Lviv after Russian drone attack on 24 March

The FSB claimed to have intercepted a consignment of heated boot inserts filled with explosives that were to have be issued to Russian soldiers fighting in Ukraine.[[310]](#cite_note-310)

In Crimea, a Russian column was attacked by Ukrainian drones. The HUR claimed that two [3M22 Zircon](/wiki/3M22_Zircon "3M22 Zircon") missiles and a [Bastion-M](/wiki/K-300P_Bastion-P "K-300P Bastion-P") launcher were destroyed and another launcher was damaged.[[311]](#cite_note-311)

A Russian drone strike damaged the bell tower of the [Bernardine Monastery](/wiki/Bernardine_Church,_Lviv "Bernardine Church, Lviv") in Lviv, as well as a nearby residential building. Another residential building was damaged by a drone in [Sykhiv](/wiki/Sykhivskyi_District "Sykhivskyi District") district. According to governor [Maksym Kozytskyi](/wiki/Maksym_Kozytskyi "Maksym Kozytskyi"), several buildings were damaged by drones and debris in [Komarno](/wiki/Komarno_urban_hromada "Komarno urban hromada"), [Dobrosyn-Maheriv](/wiki/Dobrosyn-Maheriv_settlement_hromada "Dobrosyn-Maheriv settlement hromada") and [Bibrka](/wiki/Bibrka_urban_hromada "Bibrka urban hromada") [hromadas](/wiki/Hromada "Hromada") of [Lviv Oblast](/wiki/Lviv_Oblast "Lviv Oblast"). In total, 27 people were wounded as a result of the attacks in Lviv.[[312]](#cite_note-312)

### 25 March

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-26_Ust-Luga.png/250px-NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-26_Ust-Luga.png)](/wiki/File:NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-26_Ust-Luga.png)

[NASA](/wiki/NASA "NASA")'s [FIRMS](/wiki/Fire_Information_for_Resource_Management_System "Fire Information for Resource Management System") detected fires on 26 March 2026 01:30:00 ([UTC](/wiki/UTC "UTC")) at the [Ust-Luga Multimodal Complex](/wiki/Ust-Luga_Multimodal_Complex "Ust-Luga Multimodal Complex")

Ukrainian missiles seriously damaged energy infrastructure facilities in Belgorod, according to the regional governor [Vyacheslav Gladkov](/wiki/Vyacheslav_Gladkov "Vyacheslav Gladkov"), causing power outages and affecting other services.[[313]](#cite_note-313) A shipbuilder in [Vyborg](/wiki/Vyborg "Vyborg") was attacked by Ukrainian drones, damaging a [Project 23550 patrol ship](/wiki/Project_23550_patrol_ship "Project 23550 patrol ship").[[314]](#cite_note-314) The port of the [Ust-Luga Multimodal Complex](/wiki/Ust-Luga_Multimodal_Complex "Ust-Luga Multimodal Complex") was also attacked, a fire broke out according to regional governor [Aleksandr Drozdenko](/wiki/Aleksandr_Drozdenko "Aleksandr Drozdenko").[[315]](#cite_note-315)

### 26 March

The Sierra Leone-flagged, but Turkey-operated crude oil tanker *Altura* (IMO 9292199) sailing from the Russian port of Novorossiysk was attacked just outside Turkish waters, 33 km (21 mi) off the [Bosporus](/wiki/Bosporus "Bosporus"), by an alleged naval drone strike which damaged the vessel's deck, bridge and engine room. The vessel, which was sanctioned by Ukraine, Switzerland and the United Kingdom, started taking on water after the strike but the crew of 27 were unharmed. The vessel had 140,000 tons of oil aboard at the time and regularly travelled from Novorossiysk to Georgia and India according to the HUR.[[316]](#cite_note-316)[[317]](#cite_note-317)

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/84/NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-26_Kinef.png/250px-NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-26_Kinef.png)](/wiki/File:NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-26_Kinef.png)

NASA's FIRMS detected extensive fire on 26 March 2026 00:37:00 (UTC) at [Kinef](/wiki/Kinef "Kinef") (The topmost detection is consistent with refinery [gas flares](/wiki/Gas_flare "Gas flare"))

The [Kirishi oil refinery](/wiki/Kirishi_oil_refinery "Kirishi oil refinery") was attacked for a second night in a row by Ukrainian drones. Flights out of [Pulkovo Airport](/wiki/Pulkovo_Airport "Pulkovo Airport") were also suspended again. Leningrad Governor [Aleksandr Drozdenko](/wiki/Aleksandr_Drozdenko "Aleksandr Drozdenko") claimed over 20 drones were shot down but there was “damage in the industrial zone”. [NASA FIRMS](/wiki/Fire_Information_for_Resource_Management_System "Fire Information for Resource Management System") reported a fire at the oil refinery.[[318]](#cite_note-318)[[319]](#cite_note-319)

Ukrainian security services supplied Russian drone pilots with booby trapped spools of fibre-optic cable, killing five Russian UAV operators and injuring four, according to intercepts of Russian radio traffic.[[320]](#cite_note-320)

The [Institute for the Study of War](/wiki/Institute_for_the_Study_of_War "Institute for the Study of War") reported that Russian forces had seized the villages of Kalenyky and Riznykivka in Donetsk Oblast at a prior date, citing geolocated footage.[[321]](#cite_note-321)

### 27 March

Drones attacked Primorsk and [Ust-Luga](/wiki/Ust-Luga "Ust-Luga") for a third consecutive night, with locals reporting explosions in both cities. Pulkovo Airport again suspended flights during the attack. Russian officials claimed to have destroyed some 20 aerial targets.[[322]](#cite_note-322) In neighbouring [Vologda Oblast](/wiki/Vologda_Oblast "Vologda Oblast") drones attacked the [Cherepovets](/wiki/Cherepovets "Cherepovets") chemical factory. Governor [Georgy Filimonov](/wiki/Georgy_Filimonov "Georgy Filimonov") confirmed on Telegram that 10 drones had been shot down, while "eight strikes had been recorded".[[323]](#cite_note-323)

### 28 March

[![](//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ee/NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-28_Yaroslavl.png/250px-NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-28_Yaroslavl.png)](/wiki/File:NASA_FIRMS_2026-03-28_Yaroslavl.png)

NASA's FIRMS detected an extensive fire on 28 March 2026 00:33:00 (UTC) at [Yaroslavl refinery](/wiki/Yaroslavl_refinery "Yaroslavl refinery")

Ukrainian drones started a fire at the [Yaroslavl refinery](/wiki/Yaroslavl_refinery "Yaroslavl refinery"), one of Russia's five largest refineries. The [Governor of Yaroslavl Oblast](/wiki/Governor_of_Yaroslavl_Oblast "Governor of Yaroslavl Oblast"), [Mikhail Yevrayev](/wiki/Mikhail_Yevrayev "Mikhail Yevrayev"), claimed that Ukrainian drones struck a private home, killing a child and injuring three others.[[324]](#cite_note-324) [[325]](#cite_note-325) As many as 155 Ukrainian drones attacked 16 Russian oblasts as well as Crimea, according to the Russian Ministry of Defence.[[326]](#cite_note-326) [FP-5 Flamingo](/wiki/FP-5_Flamingo "FP-5 Flamingo") missiles destroyed the JSC Promsintez explosives plant in [Chapaevsk](/wiki/Chapaevsk "Chapaevsk"), [Samara Oblast](/wiki/Samara_Oblast "Samara Oblast"). [[327]](#cite_note-327) Subsequent satellite images showed that the attack "failed to deliver direct structural penetration of the production halls". One missile was shot down "at the last moment", a second struck a lightning rod tower and the third missile detonated close potentially causing "minor peripheral damage".[[328]](#cite_note-328)

### 29 March

Ukrainian drones attacked the port of [Ust-Luga](/wiki/Ust-Luga "Ust-Luga"), causing a fire according to the Leningrad governor. He also claimed 27 drones had been destroyed.[[329]](#cite_note-329) Ukrainian drones attacked [Taganrog](/wiki/Taganrog "Taganrog"), with reports of explosions. One resident was reported killed, another injured while a drone hit a school.[[330]](#cite_note-330)

Fighters of Ukraine's [43rd Mechanized Brigade](/wiki/43rd_Mechanized_Brigade_(Ukraine) "43rd Mechanized Brigade (Ukraine)") reported the death of four Russian soldiers and capture of six others from the 153rd Tank Regiment during the previous three days in the area of [Kivsharivka](/wiki/Kivsharivka "Kivsharivka").[[331]](#cite_note-331)

### 30 March

Ukrainian drones attacked a chemical factory in [Tolyatti](/wiki/Tolyatti "Tolyatti"), [Samara Oblast](/wiki/Samara_Oblast "Samara Oblast").[[332]](#cite_note-332)

Ukraine has started using private companies to shoot down drones, with the purpose of reducing the use of Ukrainian troops.[[333]](#cite_note-333)

### 31 March

A Russian military [An-26](/wiki/An-26 "An-26") crashed in Crimea killing 29 onboard, including Lieutenant General Alexander Otroshchenko commander of [45th Air and Air Defence Forces Army](/wiki/45th_Air_and_Air_Defence_Forces_Army "45th Air and Air Defence Forces Army").[[334]](#cite_note-334) An [Su-34](/wiki/Su-34 "Su-34") was also lost in an unspecified location killing both crew, the cause of both crashes are under investigation. The An-26 "was not hit" with Russian officials blaming the crew, however one Russian source stated that the Su-34 was intercepting a drone when it went down.[[335]](#cite_note-335)

April 2026
----------

### 1 April

The Russian Ministry of Defence claimed to have seized all of Luhansk Oblast, which Ukrainian officials denied.[[336]](#cite_note-336)

The Ukrainian General Staff claimed to have struck the Strela JSC in Bryansk Oblast, claiming that the company produced "components for cruise missiles".[[337]](#cite_note-337)

### 2 April

Ukrainian drones struck the Bashneft-Novoil oil refinery in [Ufa](/wiki/Ufa "Ufa"), starting a fire.[[338]](#cite_note-338) Ukrainian drones destroyed a [An-72P](/wiki/An-72 "An-72") maritime patrol aircraft and several radars at the [Kirovske military airfield](/wiki/Kirovske_(air_base) "Kirovske (air base)"), south of Islam-Terek, Crimea.[[339]](#cite_note-339)
