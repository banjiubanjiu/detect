# Ukraine’s National Guards destroy Pantsir-S system deep behind enemy lines

According to Ukrinform, this was reported by National Guard Commander Major General Oleksandr Pivnenko on Telegram, where he also shared a video of the combat operation.

“Another significant enemy target has been destroyed. Pilots of the NGU 23rd Khortytsia Brigade detected and eliminated a Pantsir-S air defense missile and gun system deep behind enemy lines,” he said.

Pivnenko emphasized that this is part of systematic work delivering tangible results: one more element of the enemy’s air defense has been eliminated, significantly reducing its combat capabilities.
