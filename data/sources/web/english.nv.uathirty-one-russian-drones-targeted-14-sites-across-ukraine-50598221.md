# Ukraine downs 77 of 110 Russian attack drones

April 7, 2026, 06:30 AM

![Mobile fire group (Photo: REUTERS/Stanislav Kozliuk)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/270/125/original/9a0278128a36188b291fa0254f37ebb2.png?q=85&stamp=20260407093139&w=900&f=webp)

Mobile fire group (Photo: REUTERS/Stanislav Kozliuk)

Russian forces launched 110 Shahed, Gerbera, Italmas, and other types of attack drones at Ukraine overnight from six locations in Russia and temporarily occupied territories, the Air Force [reported](https://t.me/kpszsu/59626) on Telegram on April 7.

As of the morning, Ukrainian air defenses, aviation, anti-aircraft missile forces, electronic warfare units, unmanned systems, and mobile fire groups had shot down or suppressed 77 of the drones across the north, south, and east of the country.

Thirty-one of the enemy drones targeted 14 sites across Ukraine. Nine other sites reported damage
