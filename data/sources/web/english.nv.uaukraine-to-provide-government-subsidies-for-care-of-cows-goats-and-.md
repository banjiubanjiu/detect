# Ukraine launches subsidies for cow, goat and sheep farmers in 2026

April 7, 2026, 05:26 PM

![Image 6: Ukraine has launched a selection process for government subsidies for the care of cows, goats, and sheep (Photo: JACLOU-DL/Pixabay)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/270/477/original/45018cc8f9f5b10cad8ddeed5237a3b4.png?q=85&stamp=20260407145655&w=900&f=webp)

Ukraine has launched a selection process for government subsidies for the care of cows, goats, and sheep (Photo: JACLOU-DL/Pixabay)

Ukraine has begun accepting applications through the State Agrarian Register for special budget subsidies to support cattle, goat and sheep farming, the Ministry of Economy, Environment and Agriculture [announced](https://me.gov.ua/News/Detail/ef1d0402-5b58-451e-bf22-ce3dbf668410?lang=uk-UA&title=Dar) on April 7.

Farmers can receive:

*   7,000 hryvnias ($161) per cow for herds of 3 to 100 animals, and
*   2,000 hryvnias ($46) per breeding goat or ewe for herds of 5 to 500 animals.

“Recipients are eligible to receive one-time support in only one of these areas,” the Ministry of Economy stated. “In 2026, business entities will be eligible for the subsidy regardless of whether they received a special budget subsidy in 2022–2025.”

In November 2025, the Cabinet of Ministers increased the partial reimbursement rate for the construction costs of livestock farms for raising cattle in frontline areas from 25% to 50% of the total cost.

In April2025, the Ukrainian State Fund for Support of Farming Enterprises disbursed the first payments of a special budget subsidy to farming enterprises and other agricultural producers.

A total of 7,766 farming enterprises and other agricultural producers received subsidies amounting to 386.5 million hryvnias ($8.9 million).
