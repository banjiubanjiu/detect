# Russians carry out 788 strikes on Zaporizhzhia region, injuring three people

According to Ukrinform, the head of the Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration, Ivan Fedorov, reported this on Telegram.

Three men were injured as a result of enemy attacks in the Zaporizhzhia and Polohy districts, the statement said.

According to the regional administration, Russian forces launched 788 strikes over the past day on 43 settlements in the Zaporizhzhia region.

In particular, Russian troops carried out 17 airstrikes on Hryhorivka, Veselianka, Krynytsia, Zhovtenke, Tersyanka, Huliaipilske, Vozdvyzhivka, Dolynka, Kopani, Verkhni Tersa, Shyroke, Tsvitkove, and Svoboda.

A total of 570 UAVs of various types (mostly FPV drones) attacked Vilniansk, Kushuhum, Komyshuvakha, Tavriiske, Bilenke, Rozumivka, Lysohirka, Novooleksandrivka, Hryhorivka, Novoyakovlivka, Stepnohirsk, Stepove, Pavlivka, Prymorske, Lukianivske, Orikhiv, Huliaipole, Zaliznychne, Shcherbaky, Novodanylivka, Novoandriivka, Mala Tokmachka, Charivne, Dobropillia, Sviatopetrivka, Pryluky, Preobrazhenka, Huliaipilske, Staroukrainka, and Olenokostiantynivka.

Four MLRS strikes were recorded on Hirke, Charivne, Mala Tokmachka, and Novopavlivka. Additionally, 197 artillery strikes hit Stepnohirsk, Prymorske, Stepove, Pavlivka, Lukianivske, Orikhiv, Huliaipole, Zaliznychne, Shcherbaky, Novoandriivka, Novodanylivka, Mala Tokmachka, Charivne, Dobropillia, Huliaipilske, Staroukrainka, and Olenokostiantynivka.

Authorities received 73 reports of damage to infrastructure, homes, and vehicles.

As Ukrinform previously reported, Russian forces also attacked a car with a drone in the Polohy district of Zaporizhzhia region, injuring two people.
