# Russian army strikes 30 settlements in Sumy region over past day, leaving one killed and three injured

photos

Ukrinform

Over the past day, Russian forces carried out attacks on 30 settlements in the Sumy region. One person was killed and three others were wounded.

According to Ukrinform, the National Police of Ukraine reported this on its website.

In the Romny community, a 42-year-old man was killed as a result of an enemy UAV strike. Women aged 47, 61, and 66 were injured. A private house was damaged.

In the Sumy, Shostka, Velyka Pysarivka, and Berezivka communities, apartment buildings and private homes were damaged.

As Ukrinform reported, on the evening of April 7, Russian troops attacked the Romny community in the Sumy region. A man was killed after a strike hit a residential building, and there were also injuries.
