“A whole civilization will die tonight” and 47 years of “extortion, corruption, and death” will finally end, U.S. President Donald Trump said in a [Truth Social](https://truthsocial.com/@realDonaldTrump) post on April 7, escalating his threats toward Iran.

“A whole civilization will die tonight, never to be brought back again. I don’t want that to happen, but it probably will. However, now that we have Complete and Total Regime Change, where different, smarter, and less radicalized minds prevail, maybe something revolutionarily wonderful can happen, WHO KNOWS? We will find out tonight, one of the most important moments in the long and complex history of the World. 47 years of extortion, corruption, and death, will finally end. God Bless the Great People of Iran!” Trump wrote.

Trump had earlier said Iran could be destroyed in a single night, and that such a strike could come as soon as April 7.

The U.S. president has also repeatedly warned that the United States could strike power plants, bridges and other infrastructure in Iran if Tehran does not reach a deal or reopen the Strait of Hormuz.

Trump had written that Iran must reopen the strait by 8 p.m. Eastern on April 7, which was 3 a.m. in Kyiv on April 8.

Tehran responded by warning that if the United States launches further strikes on Iranian infrastructure, “the gates of hell” will open for the United States and Israel. CNN reported that Iran said it would fully reopen the Strait of Hormuz only after receiving compensation for financial losses caused by the war.

On April 6, Axios reported that the United States and Iran were discussing through mediators the terms of a potential 45-day ceasefire that could lead to a final end to the war.
