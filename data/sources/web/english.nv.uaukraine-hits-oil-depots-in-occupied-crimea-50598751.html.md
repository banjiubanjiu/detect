# Ukraine confirms strike on oil depot in occupied Feodosia – General Staff

April 8, 2026, 04:49 PM

![Image 6: Fire in occupied Feodosia on the night of April 8 (Photo: Crimean wind/Telegram)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/271/281/original/af8ebc3badafb896f58f8468810c7dd8.png?q=85&stamp=20260408165044&w=900&f=webp)

Fire in occupied Feodosia on the night of April 8 (Photo: Crimean wind/Telegram)

Ukraine’s Defense Forces struck oil depots in Feodosia and Hvardiiske in occupied Crimea overnight, [the General Staff said](https://t.me/GeneralStaffZSU/37055)on Apr. 8.

They said Russian forces store fuel and lubricants at these oil depots to supply their troops.

Ukrainian forces also struck Russian logistics depots in the areas of Svitloye in temporarily occupied Zaporizhzhia Oblast, Sukhodilsk in Luhansk Oblast, and Velyka Novosilka in Donetsk Oblast.

In addition, Ukraine’s Defense Forces hit a field artillery depot near Yalta and a UAV storage site near Stepne in Donetsk Oblast. An ammunition depot was also struck near the settlement of Uralo-Kavkaz in Luhansk Oblast.

The General Staff said Ukrainian forces also targeted a radio-electronic intelligence station near Novoozerne and a coastal missile system Bastion position near Sofiivka in temporarily occupied Crimea.

Russian losses are still being assessed, the General Staff added.

Earlier, Telegram channels reported that drones attacked Feodosia overnight into April 8, with reports of a strike and a fire at a local oil depot.

The same day, Commander of Ukraine’s Unmanned Systems Forces Robert “Madyar” Brovdi said the Marine Oil Terminal in temporarily occupied Feodosia had been hit.
