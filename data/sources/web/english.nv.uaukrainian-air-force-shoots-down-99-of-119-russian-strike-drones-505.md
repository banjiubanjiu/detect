Ukrainian air defense eliminated 99 enemy UAVs (Photo: National Guard's Northern tactical-operational grouping/Facebook)

Russia's army deployed 119 strike UAVs of the Shahed, Gerbera, Italmaz, and other types against Ukraine overnight, Air Force [reported](https://t.me/kpszsu/59722) on April 9.

About 70 of the drones that attacked Ukraine were Shaheds, the military noted.

Aviation, anti-aircraft missile troops, electronic warfare and unmanned systems units, and mobile fire groups of the Ukrainian Defense Forces repelled the air attack.

Ukrainian air defense neutralized 99 Russian UAVs of various types as of 8 a.m.

Ad

Hits by 16 drones were recorded at 11 locations, along with falling debris from downed targets in four other places, Air Force added.

The air attack is ongoing, with several enemy drones remaining in the airspace, the military warned.
