# Russians attack bus in Nikopol district again, injuries reported

photo, video

Ukrinform

Russian invaders attacked a passenger bus in the Nikopol district for the second time today. Five people were injured.

This was reported on Telegram by Oleksandr Hanzha, head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Military Administration, according to Ukrinform.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/thumb_files/630_360_1775549908-5587.jpeg)

“Five people were injured. The Russians hit a passenger bus in the Nikopol district for the second time today,” he wrote.

**Chernihiv**region; casualties reported

The attack took place in the Chervonohryhorivka community. A minibus serving an intercity route was damaged there. A woman and four men were injured. Medical personnel are providing the victims with all necessary assistance.

Earlier it was reported that on Tuesday morning, the enemy struck a bus in Nikopol. Four people were killed and 16 were injured.
