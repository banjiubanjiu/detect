# UK Foreign Minister says Israeli assault on Lebanon 'deeply damaging'

9 April 2026 07:21 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/israel-attacks-beirut-lebanon-9-april-2026-reuters.JPG.jpg?itok=6xwDz_3X)

British Foreign Secretary Yvette Cooper described Israel's attacks across Lebanon on Wednesday as "deeply damaging", stressing a need for resolution to hostilities in the region.

"We want to see Lebanon included in the ceasefire... We want it extended to cover Lebanon, because otherwise that will destabilise the whole region," Cooper told Times Radio.

"That escalation that we saw from Israel yesterday was deeply damaging, and we want to see an end to hostilities," she added.
