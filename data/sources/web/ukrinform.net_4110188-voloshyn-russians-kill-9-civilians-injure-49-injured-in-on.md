# Voloshyn: Russians kill 9 civilians, injure 49 injured in one day in southern regions

The spokesperson for the Southern Defense Forces, Vladyslav Voloshyn, reported this to Ukrinform

"Russians in the south have effectively unleashed terror against the peaceful local population, striking civilians in cities and villages, even hunting, for example in Nikopol, public transport. As of 17:30, 49 people injured is the highest number in recent times," he said.

*The Russian army struck a minibus with a drone in Nikopol / Photo: State Emergency Service*

According to the spokesperson, settlements in the Dnipropetrovsk, Zaporizhzhia, Odesa, and Kherson regions came under attack. In particular, in Nikopol, a UAV strike damaged an administrative building, residential houses, cars, and a bus; 18 people were injured and 4 killed. In Kherson, shelling destroyed a pharmacy, killing 4 people and injuring 11. In Romanky, Dnipropetrovsk region, a child was killed and three people were injured as a result of a UAV strike.

Significant destruction of civilian infrastructure was also recorded: in Vylkove, a three-story administrative building, a hangar, and garages were destroyed; in Vilniansk, a residential building and outbuildings were damaged. In Stepanivka, a hospital and a school were damaged, and three people were injured, including a child. In a number of settlements, including Bilenke, Lysohirka, Nyzhnia Khortytsia, Tavriiske, and Stryukivka, civilians were injured and homes, transport, and facilities were damaged.

*Photo: Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration*

As reported by Ukrinform, Russian forces attacked a passenger bus for the second time in one day in the Nikopol district. Five people were injured.
