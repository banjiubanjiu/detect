# Report: Surviving the sexual violence crisis in Darfur

![Image 5](https://img.msf.org/AssetLink/2w76lor8462qo82spd01xqghfl0bf13s.jpg)

Illustration used to accompany MSF's report on sexual violence in Darfur, "There is something that I want to tell you...": Surviving the sexual violence crisis in Darfur.

Médecins Sans Frontières' (MSF's) latest report, _“There is something I want to tell you…”: Surviving the sexual violence crisis in Darfur_, provides the most comprehensive documented accounts of [sexual violence](https://www.msf.org/sexual-violence "Sexual violence") in [Sudan’s](https://www.msf.org/sudan "Sudan")[war](https://www.msf.org/conflict-sudan "Conflict in Sudan"), with victim and survivor testimonies and data from MSF medical programmes highlighting clear patterns of widespread and systematic abuse.
