# Israeli army warns Lebanon town residents to evacuate amid strikes

8 April 2026 07:19 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/People%20gather%20after%20a%20two-week%20ceasefire%20in%20the%20Iran%20war%20was%20announced%2C%20in%20Tehran.JPG.jpg?itok=sRPaddf1)

The Israeli military issued an urgent warning for residents of Tyre, particularly in the Shabriha and al-Abbasiya areas of south Lebanon, to evacuate and move north of the Zahrani River.

The warning came as strikes and raids continued in southern Lebanon.

Israeli air strikes were also reported in the towns of Srifa and near Sharqiya, according to Al Jazeera.
