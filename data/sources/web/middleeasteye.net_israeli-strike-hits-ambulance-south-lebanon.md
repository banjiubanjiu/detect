# Israeli strike hits ambulance in south Lebanon

8 April 2026 04:48 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/People%20gather%20after%20a%20two-week%20ceasefire%20in%20the%20Iran%20war%20was%20announced%2C%20in%20Tehran.JPG.jpg?itok=sRPaddf1)

An Israeli air raid targeted an ambulance in the town of Qlaileh near the coastal city of Tyre, several Lebanese media outlets reported.

Lebanese health officials have said Israeli forces have repeatedly targeted medics and rescue crews during the conflict.
