# SBU exposes FSB agent who was passing on coordinates of warehouses and military equipment

![The SBU reported the arrest of a Russian agent (Photo: Security Service of Ukraine / Telegram)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/269/912/original/ec17c33395c83fec5c0f70d4ebcc2826.png?q=85&stamp=20260406183640&w=900&f=webp)

Ukraine’s SBU Security Service has detained an FSB-recruited spotter in Donetsk Oblast, who helped Russian forces adjust air strikes on Kramatorsk, including attacks with powerful FAB-1500 glide bombs, the SBU [reported](https://t.me/SBUkr/17254) on April 6.

According to the agency, the suspect — a local unemployed man recruited via Telegram job chats in October 2025 — tracked concentrations of Ukrainian troops and equipment, then sent geolocation data and screenshots to his Russian handlers.

He also gathered additional intelligence on logistics warehouses storing weapons and ammunition.

The Security Service says that to gather intelligence, the suspect surveyed the area, and whenever he identified military facilities, he marked their locations on Google Maps and saved screenshots on his personal smartphone.

In addition, according to the SBU, the agent covertly solicited information about Donetsk Oblast defenders from his acquaintances during casual conversations.

SBU reported that during a search of the detainee’s residence, a mobile phone containing evidence of his collaboration with the Russians was seized.

SBU investigators charged him under Part 2 of Article 111 of the Criminal Code of Ukraine (treason committed under martial law).

The suspect faces life imprisonment with confiscation of property.
