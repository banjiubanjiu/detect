# Germany welcomes ceasefire, urges diplomatic solution

8 April 2026 06:57 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/People%20gather%20after%20a%20two-week%20ceasefire%20in%20the%20Iran%20war%20was%20announced%2C%20in%20Tehran.JPG.jpg?itok=sRPaddf1)

German Chancellor Friedrich Merz said Wednesday that Berlin welcomes the two-week ceasefire agreed between the United States and Iran, calling it an opportunity to pursue a lasting end to the conflict.

“The aim now must be to negotiate a lasting end to the war in the coming days,” Merz said in a statement.

“This can only be achieved through diplomatic channels.”
