# Sirens sound in Israel after launch detected from Lebanon

9 April 2026 00:59 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/2026-04-08T140556Z_478277660_RC21LKAH4EP5_RTRMADP_3_IRAN-CRISIS-LEBANON-ISRAEL-STRIKE%20%281%29.JPG.jpg?itok=hq5IsAue)

Alarms blared in northern Israel after a launch was detected from Lebanon, Israeli media report.

This came after the Israeli military carried out several simultaneous strikes across the country without warning, with the capital Beirut hit by the most violent bombardment since the start of the current war.

The attack came despite a deal for a two-week ceasefire between the United States and Iran, Hezbollah's ally.
