# Malaysia-owned commercial vessel passes Hormuz

7 April 2026 05:59 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/000_A6XH2EW.jpg.jpg?itok=az6vTcg6)

One of seven Malaysian-owned commercial vessels previously stranded in the Strait of Hormuz has “been granted safe passage” and is now on its way to its final destination, Malaysia's Ministry of Foreign Affairs said.

“The positive outcome follows high-level diplomatic engagements,” including a call between Malaysian Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim and Iranian President Masoud Pezeshkian last month, the ministry said in a statement.
