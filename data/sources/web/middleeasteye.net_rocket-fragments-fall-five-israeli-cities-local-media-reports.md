# Rocket fragments fall in five Israeli cities, local media reports

7 April 2026 07:43 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/000_A6XH2EW.jpg.jpg?itok=az6vTcg6)

Rocket fragments fell in at least five cities in central Israel as explosions were heard across the region, Israeli media reported.

The Times of Israel reported that rescue forces and medics are responding to impacts in central Israel following Iran’s latest ballistic missile attack.

The Home Front Command said blasts were linked to interception activity, with loud explosions reported over Tel Aviv.

Israeli Army Radio said a rocket fired toward Eilat was a cluster munition, while the military confirmed additional missile launches from Iran toward central Israel.

Early warnings were activated, and sirens were also reported in Aqaba, Jordan, across from Eilat.
