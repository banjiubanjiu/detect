# Iran vows to ‘punish’ Israel over Lebanon strikes and ceasefire breach

9 April 2026 00:00 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/2026-04-08T140556Z_478277660_RC21LKAH4EP5_RTRMADP_3_IRAN-CRISIS-LEBANON-ISRAEL-STRIKE%20%281%29.JPG.jpg?itok=hq5IsAue)

A senior Iranian official told Al Jazeera that Tehran will “punish Israel” over what it described as violations of the ceasefire and attacks in Lebanon.

“The ceasefire includes the region, and Israel is known for breaking promises and will only be deterred by bullets,” the official said.

Separately, Fars news agency, citing a military source, reported that Iran is preparing a response to the alleged breaches.
