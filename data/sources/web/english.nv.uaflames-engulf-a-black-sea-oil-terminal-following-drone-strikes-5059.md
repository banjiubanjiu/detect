Fire in the occupied Feodosia overnight on April 8 (Photo: Crimean Wind/Telegram)

Drones attacked temporarily occupied Feodosia, with a strike and fire reported at a local oil depot, Crimean Wind Telegram channel [reported](https://t.me/Crimeanwind/96982) on the night of April 8.

The drones attacked the oil depot at 1 a.m., sparking a massive fire at the site of the strike.

Peninsula's occupation authorities were yet to report any information regarding the attack aftermath.

Drones repeatedly attacked the Marine Oil Terminal in Feodosia in November and December 2025. This terminal is a key hub for the maritime supply of fuels and lubricants to Crimea and the temporarily occupied territories of southern Ukraine.
