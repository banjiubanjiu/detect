# No Russian ships or boats present in Azov-Black Sea region, says Pletenchuk

Ukrinform

There are no enemy ships or even boats in the waters of the Azov-Black Sea region.

According to Ukrinform, Dmytro Pletenchuk, spokesperson for the Ukrainian Naval Forces, reported this on television.

“Currently, there are no enemy ships in the waters of the Azov-Black Sea region; not even boats have been deployed,” he said.

Pletenchuk noted that the enemy is stationed at its base in Novorossiysk and is not taking any action in Sevastopol.
