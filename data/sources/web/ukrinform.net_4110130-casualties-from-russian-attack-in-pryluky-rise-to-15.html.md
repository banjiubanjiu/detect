# Casualties from Russian attack in Pryluky rise to 15

This was reported by the regional police, according to Ukrinform.

“As of 5:30 p.m., there are 15 known casualties in Pryluky, including a minor girl,” law enforcement officials said.

**Chernihiv**region; casualties reported

Vyacheslav Chaus, head of the Chernihiv Regional Military Administration, reported that the injured include 11 women and 4 men. Seven people were hospitalized, three of whom were taken to the regional hospital. The rest received outpatient treatment. One woman is in serious condition; the other patients are in stable condition.

As reported, Russian forces attacked central areas in the cities of Pryluky and Novhorod-Siverskyi in Chernihiv region with drones. At least three strikes by Russian drones were recorded in Pryluky. One of the strikes hit the city council building, and two others hit other civilian administrative facilities. Five people were reported wounded.
