# Over 600 attacks on military recruiters documented since 2022

April 7, 2026, 02:28 PM

![Image 6: The highest number of attacks on military recruiters has been recorded in Kharkiv Oblast (Photo: Chernivtsi Oblast Territorial Recruitment Center via Facebook)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/270/555/original/d79c33c31b1ab3733a779763dd8cb16a.png?q=85&stamp=20260407164007&w=900&f=webp)

The highest number of attacks on military recruiters has been recorded in Kharkiv Oblast (Photo: Chernivtsi Oblast Territorial Recruitment Center via Facebook)

Ukrainian authorities have documented 611 attacks on Territorial Recruitment Center officers performing official duties since the start of Russia’s full-scale invasion on Feb. 24, 2022, the National Police [told](https://interfax.com.ua/news/general/1157326.html) the news agency Interfax-Ukraine on April 7.

Three military recruiters have been killed in such attacks. The highest numbers were recorded in Kharkiv Oblast (68), Kyiv (53), and Dnipropetrovsk Oblast (44).

Police noted a steady annual increase: five incidents in 2022, 38 in 2023, 118 in 2024, and 341 in 2025. More than 100 cases of resistance have already been recorded in 2026.

In the past week alone, three serious attacks occurred.

On April 4, two TRC officers were wounded by knife attacks in Vinnytsia.

On April 2,, a 52-year-old TRC serviceman was fatally stabbed in the neck in Lviv.

Defense Minister Mykhailo Fedorov said in February that work on a comprehensive conscription reform is ongoing.
