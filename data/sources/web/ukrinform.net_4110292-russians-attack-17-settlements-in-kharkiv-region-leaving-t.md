# Russians attack 17 settlements in Kharkiv region, leaving two civilians injured over past day

According to Ukrinform, the head of the Kharkiv Regional State Administration, Oleh Syniehubov, reported this on Facebook.

According to him, a 40-year-old man was injured in the village of Kachalivka in the Krasnokutsk community. In the village of Vysoke in the Zolochiv community, a 50-year-old woman was injured.

The enemy attacked the Kharkiv region with 5 guided aerial bombs (KABs), 24 “Geran-2” UAVs, one “Molniya” UAV, 4 FPV drones, and 37 UAVs of unidentified type.

In Bohodukhiv district, a private house (Vysoke), a warehouse (Kalinove), private houses, outbuildings, and power networks (Klynova-Novoselivka) were damaged.

In Kupiansk district, two private houses, two cars, and a building classified as a local architectural monument (Velykyi Burluk) were damaged, along with two private houses and an outbuilding (Prykolotne).

In Izium district, a car was damaged (Vasylivka Persha).

In Kharkiv district, four private houses and three cars (Merefa), as well as a warehouse (Cherkaski Tyshky), were damaged.

In Chuhuiv district, two private houses were damaged (Buhaivka).

A transit evacuation point in Lozova received 238 people over the past day. Since the beginning of its operation, 27,227 people have been registered there.

As previously reported by Ukrinform, Russian forces destroyed a 19th-century architectural monument – the Donets-Zakharzhevskyi estate in Velykyi Burluk.
