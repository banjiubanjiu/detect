# Russia strikes oil refinery in Merefa again, causing major damage

April 8, 2026, 10:37 AM

![Image 6: Rescuers extinguished a fire at a plant in Kharkiv Oblast, likely an oil refinery in Merefa, after a Russian strike on April 8, 2026 (Photo: State Emergency Service of Ukraine / Telegram)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/271/055/original/d65c7f1baf0c5f30640bf3283192c884.png?q=85&stamp=20260408123845&w=900&f=webp)

Rescuers extinguished a fire at a plant in Kharkiv Oblast, likely an oil refinery in Merefa, after a Russian strike on April 8, 2026 (Photo: State Emergency Service of Ukraine / Telegram)

Russia launched Shahed drone strikes on an oil refinery in Merefa in Kharkiv Oblast overnight, [the plant’s legal chief, Anastasiia Cherednykova, told Suspilne](https://suspilne.media/kharkiv/1284190-pidpriemstvo-zaznalo-znacnih-zbitkiv-unoci-rosijski-vijska-vdarili-po-naftopererobnomu-zavodu-u-merefi/) on Apr. 8.

She said the facility sustained significant damage, but no injuries were reported.

Cherednykova noted that this was the fifth strike on the refinery since the start of Russia’s full-scale invasion.

![Image 7: State Emergency Service of Ukraine/Telegram](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/MediaPhoto/images/000/582/983/original/57bb7d162ccfb68bbad1159592608dab.png?q=85&stamp=20260408123845&f=webp)

 Photo: State Emergency Service of Ukraine/Telegram 

She said the plant will continue operating, but it is currently unclear how long it will take to restore operations after the Russian strike.

Kharkiv regional governor Oleh Synehubov said that in Merefa, four private homes and three vehicles were damaged as a result of the attack.

The State Emergency Service said a fire broke out at a civilian enterprise in Kharkiv Oblast following a Russian drone attack. Emergency crews are working to extinguish the blaze. The incident likely concerns the refinery in Merefa.

![Image 8: State Emergency Service of Ukraine/Telegram](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/MediaPhoto/images/000/582/984/original/eb0643c46226211b6d9f100883702731.png?q=85&stamp=20260408123846&f=webp)

 Photo: State Emergency Service of Ukraine/Telegram 

Ukraine’s Air Force said Russian forces launched 176 attack UAVs overnight, including Shahed, Gerbera and Italmas drones, as well as other types of unmanned aerial vehicles. Ukraine’s air defenses neutralized 146 of them.

In March 2025, about 20 Russian drones attacked the refinery in Merefa, forcing it to completely halt operations.

The mini-refinery in Merefa belongs to the Kharkiv-based industrial and investment group AES Group. It was the largest mini-refinery in Ukraine.
