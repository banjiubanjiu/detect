# Ukraine sees growth in new truck sales – Ukrautoprom

The Ukrainian Motor Vehicle Manufacturers Association (Ukrautoprom) announced this on Telegram, Ukrinform reports.

As many as 1,085 new commercial vehicles (trucks and special-purpose vehicles) were registered in March – 23% more than in February. Compared to March 2025, demand rose by 6%.

Overall, since the beginning of the year, Ukraine's fleet of trucks and special vehicles has grown by 2,911 new units, which is nearly 3% more than in the same period last year.

Among the market leaders in March were Renault (179 vehicles), Volkswagen (112), Citroen (93), Fiat (87), and Opel (78).

In the first quarter of 2026, 15,400 new passenger cars were sold in Ukraine – an increase of 8% compared to the same period last year.
