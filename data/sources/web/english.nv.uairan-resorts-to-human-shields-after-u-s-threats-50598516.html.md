“On Tuesday at 2 p.m., at power plants across the country we will stand shoulder to shoulder to say: attacking civilian infrastructure is a war crime,” Rahimi said.

Iran’s Mehr news agency later published photos and video showing people forming human chains around several power stations and on a bridge in Ahvaz.
