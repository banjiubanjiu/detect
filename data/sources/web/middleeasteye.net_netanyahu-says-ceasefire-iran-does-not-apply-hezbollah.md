# Netanyahu says ceasefire with Iran does not apply to Hezbollah

8 April 2026 18:46 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/2026-04-08T140556Z_478277660_RC21LKAH4EP5_RTRMADP_3_IRAN-CRISIS-LEBANON-ISRAEL-STRIKE%20%281%29.JPG.jpg?itok=hq5IsAue)

Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu said any ceasefire with Iran will not extend to Hezbollah, adding that Israeli forces will continue their attacks on the group.

“A ceasefire with Iran will not include Hezbollah. We are continuing to hit Hezbollah,” he said.

Referring to the latest Israeli assault on Lebanon, Netanyahu said: “We have hit locations Hezbollah thought they were safe [in].”
