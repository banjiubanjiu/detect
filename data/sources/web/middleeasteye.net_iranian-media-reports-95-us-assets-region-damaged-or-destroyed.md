# Iranian media reports 95 percent of US assets in region 'damaged or destroyed'

8 April 2026 06:28 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/People%20gather%20after%20a%20two-week%20ceasefire%20in%20the%20Iran%20war%20was%20announced%2C%20in%20Tehran.JPG.jpg?itok=sRPaddf1)

Iranian state-linked media reported claims that a large share of US military assets in the region had been destroyed, citing a political analyst.

The remarks, carried by Tasnim News, said “95% of American assets in the region” had been damaged or destroyed, including bases, aircraft, drones and related infrastructure.

The report also said significant damage had been inflicted on Israel, asserting that Iran and its allies were gaining the upper hand in the conflict.
