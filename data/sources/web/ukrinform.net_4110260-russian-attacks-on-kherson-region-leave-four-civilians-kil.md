# Russian attacks on Kherson region leave four civilians killed, 21 injured over past day

According to Ukrinform, this was reported on Telegram by the head of the regional military administration, Oleksandr Prokudin.

He said that the settlements of Tomyna Balka, Beryslav, Bilozerka, Zolota Balka, Ukrainska, Stepanivka, Mykilske, Novoraisk, Antonivka, Burhunka, Vesеlе, Havrylivka, Dniprovske, Kizomys, Lvivske, Novotiahynka, Osokorivka, Prydniprovske, Tokarivka, Chornobaivka, Sofiivka, Ivanivka, Mykhailivka, Novokairy, Novotiahynka, and Kherson itself came under enemy drone terror, airstrikes, and artillery shelling.

Seventeen multi-story buildings, 28 private houses, a pharmacy, an outbuilding, a mobile communication tower, a warehouse, a private garage, vehicles, and an ambulance were damaged.

According to Prokudin, one person was evacuated from liberated communities in the region yesterday.
