# Trump threatens 50% tariffs on countries supplying Iran with weapons

8 April 2026 23:37 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/2026-04-08T140556Z_478277660_RC21LKAH4EP5_RTRMADP_3_IRAN-CRISIS-LEBANON-ISRAEL-STRIKE%20%281%29.JPG.jpg?itok=hq5IsAue)

US President Donald Trump said countries supplying Iran with military equipment will face immediate 50 percent tariffs on all goods entering the United States.

In a social media post, Trump said the measures would take effect immediately, with no exemptions.

The threat comes hours after a two-week ceasefire was agreed with Tehran.

China and Russia have been accused of helping Iran build its military capabilities, though both have denied providing weapons recently.
