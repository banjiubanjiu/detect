# Shakhtar’s Brazilian winger Pedrinho ready to play for Ukraine

April 8, 2026, 11:11 AM

![Image 6: Pedrinho is a Brazilian football player for Shakhtar (Photo: instagram.com/pedrovictor38)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/271/075/original/33f932ecb5e884a313e8b4c2543e1b1e.png?q=85&stamp=20260408130132&w=900&f=webp)

Pedrinho is a Brazilian football player for Shakhtar (Photo: instagram.com/pedrovictor38)

Shakhtar Donetsk Brazilian winger Pedrinho is ready to play for Ukraine’s national team if given the opportunity, he said during an online meeting with children participating in the Pace for Health project on April 7, [describing](https://www.instagram.com/p/DW0rEtKDABl/) it as an “incredible chance.”

The project, run by the Shakhtar Social foundation under the Erasmus+ Sport program, aims to support psychosocial recovery and development of children through football. 55 children from various Ukrainian cities joined the session.

Responding to a question from the young participants, Pedrinho said: “I have heard a lot of talk about this. It would be an incredible chance. I’m really interested in such an offer and I think I would accept it. I would be happy to help the Ukraine national team. This country has become very close to me during this time. However, I want to emphasize that so far all these talks remain only rumors.”

Rumors about possible naturalization for Pedrinho and fellow Brazilian Pedro Henrique have circulated this year, with Ukraine head coach Sergiy Rebrov previously commenting on the speculation.

Pedrinho joined Shakhtar from Benfica in July 2021 for €18 million ($21 million). He spent time on loan at Brazil’s Atlético Mineiro from summer 2022 to June 2024.

This season he has played 32 club matches, scoring seven goals and providing nine assists.
