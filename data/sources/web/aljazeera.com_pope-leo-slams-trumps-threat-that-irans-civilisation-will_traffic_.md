# Pope Leo slams Trump’s threat that Iran’s ‘civilisation will die’

Pope Leo condemned Donald Trump’s threats that Iran’s “civilisation will die” if the Strait of Hormuz remained closed, calling the US president’s remarks “unacceptable”. The comments come hours before Trump’s deadline, after which he has threatened strikes on Iranian power plants if no deal is reached.
