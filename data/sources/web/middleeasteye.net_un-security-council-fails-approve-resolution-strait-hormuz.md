# UN Security Council fails to approve resolution on Strait of Hormuz

7 April 2026 17:08 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/iran%20university%20bomb%20reuters.jpg.jpg?itok=YPsB7zM9)

At a vote in the UN Security Council on Tuesday, China and Russia vetoed a Bahraini resolution encouraging states to coordinate efforts to protect commercial shipping in the Strait of Hormuz.

The 15-member Security Council voted 11 in favor of the resolution, with two against and two abstentions.
