# Israel renews attacks on southern Lebanon

9 April 2026 06:19 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/israel-attacks-beirut-lebanon-9-april-2026-reuters.JPG.jpg?itok=6xwDz_3X)

Israel is launching air strikes on southern Lebanon, Al Jazeera reports.

This follows a series of Israeli attacks across the country, in what news reports have described as the deadliest wave of strikes on the country since the war on Iran began.

At least 250 people were killed, with over a thousand more left wounded as a result
