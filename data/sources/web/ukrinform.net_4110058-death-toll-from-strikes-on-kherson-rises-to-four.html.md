# Death toll from strikes on Kherson rises to four

Ukrinform

The death toll from Russian strikes on the Korabelny district of Kherson has risen to four.

This was reported on Telegram by Alexander Prokudin, head of the Kherson Regional Military Administration, according to Ukrinform.

“The death toll from Russian strikes on the Korabelny district of Kherson has risen to four. It has been reported that another man sustained fatal injuries as a result of the attack,” Prokudin said.

He noted that the identity of the deceased is being established.

As reported by Ukrinform, in the Korabelny district of Kherson, three people were killed and seven were wounded as a result of Russian strikes on residential buildings.
