# Russian drone attacks ambulance in Kherson, injuring three medics

The Kherson City Military Administration reported this on Telegram, according to Ukrinform.

"At around 19:00, Russian occupiers attacked an emergency team with a drone in the Dnipro district," the statement said.

According to the administration, a 48-year-old doctor, a 24-year-old paramedic, and a 42-year-old emergency medical technician were hospitalized with concussions, blast injuries, and closed traumatic brain injuries. Further examinations are ongoing.

The Kherson City Military Administration also reported that at around 18:00 in the same district, two medical workers were injured as a result of Russian shelling.

A 26-year-old male doctor and a 25-year-old female doctor sought medical assistance with blast injuries and shrapnel wounds. They will receive outpatient treatment.

As Ukrinform previously reported, on April 7 Russian forces shelled a private residential area in the Dnipro district of Kherson with artillery. A building and a vehicle caught fire.
