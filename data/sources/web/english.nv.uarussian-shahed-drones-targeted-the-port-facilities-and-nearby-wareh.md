# Russian drone barrage hits port in Odesa Oblast

April 8, 2026, 06:28 AM

![Image 6: Fire after Russian drone attack on Odesa Oblast, April 8 (Photo: Odesa Oblast's SES)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/270/866/original/fdebc11da7ba4f9ba29ce52019b6ff83.png?q=85&stamp=20260408092856&w=900&f=webp)

Fire after Russian drone attack on Odesa Oblast, April 8 (Photo: Odesa Oblast's SES)

Russian forces launched a large-scale overnight drone attack on Ukraine’s southern Odesa Oblast, damaging port infrastructure and sparking fires, regional Governor Oleh Kiper [reported](https://t.me/odeskaODA/15258) early on April 8, sharing photos of the aftermath on his Telegram account.

According to Ukraine’s State Emergency Service, the Iranian-designed Shahed drones targeted the port facilities and nearby warehouses. First responders extinguished numerous blazes at the site of the attack.

The strikes partially destroyed a warehouse and an auxiliary structure.

There have been no reported casualties.
