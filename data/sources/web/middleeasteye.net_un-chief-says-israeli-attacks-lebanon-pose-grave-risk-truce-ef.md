# UN chief says Israeli attacks on Lebanon pose 'grave risk' to truce efforts

9 April 2026 04:38 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/israel-attacks-beirut-lebanon-9-april-2026-reuters.JPG.jpg?itok=6xwDz_3X)

The United Nations secretary-general warned that Israel's ongoing assault on Lebanon "poses a grave risk" to the US-Iran ceasefire deal, his spokesperson said in a statement.

"The ongoing military activity in Lebanon poses a grave risk to the ceasefire and the efforts toward a lasting and comprehensive peace in the region. The Secretary-General reiterates his call to all parties to immediately cease hostilities," UN chief Antonio Guterres's spokesman said.
