Ukrainian Military Intelligence (HUR) forces struck the Russian railway ferry Slavyanin in the Kerch Strait with drones on the night of April 6, HUR [reported](https://t.me/DIUkraine/8162) on April 8.

This was the last Russian railway ferry remaining afloat in temporarily occupied Kerch in Crimea, and the vessel was disabled as a result of the attack, intelligence data indicated.

![Image 7: HUR](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/MediaPhoto/images/000/582/997/original/65b36085007e808f6e05ff0607e940a7.png?q=85&stamp=20260408141331&f=webp)

 Photo: HUR 

Slavyanin transported fuels and lubricants, weapons, military equipment, and ammunition to Russian-occupied Crimea.

HUR forces earlier attacked Slavyanin ferry on March 14, 2026. Special forces also disabled another occupiers' railway ferry, the Avangard, at that time.
