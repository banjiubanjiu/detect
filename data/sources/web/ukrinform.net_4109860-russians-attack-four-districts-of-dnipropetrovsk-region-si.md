# Russians attack four districts of Dnipropetrovsk region since yesterday evening, leaving one dead and five injured

According to Ukrinform, the head of the regional military administration, Oleksandr Hanzha, reported this on Telegram.

In the Pokrovske community of Synelnykove district, a private house caught fire. Three other homes and a car were also damaged.

The 11-year-old boy died. Women aged 31 and 61, and a 33-year-old man, were hospitalized in moderate condition.

In Pavlohrad district, Pavlohrad city and the Bohdanivka community were hit. A fire broke out, and an administrative building and power lines were damaged.

Two men aged 52 and 66 were injured and hospitalized in moderate condition. In Apostolove of Kryvyi Rih district, Russians damaged infrastructure.

In Nikopol, Chervonohryhorivka, and Marhanets communities of Nikopol district, an administrative building, a private house, and a car were damaged.

As Ukrinform reported, ten residents of the Dnipropetrovsk region were affected by Russian shelling on April 6.
