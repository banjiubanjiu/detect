# Russian forces attack Zaporizhzhia region with drones and airstrikes, casualties reported

Ukrinform

Throughout the day, the enemy attacked settlements in the Zaporizhzhia district. Three people were wounded as a result of the attacks.

Ivan Fedorov, head of the Zaporizhzhia Regional Military Administration, reported this on Telegram, according to Ukrinform.

“Three people were wounded as a result of enemy attacks on the Zaporizhzhia district,” he wrote.

According to the head of the Regional Military Administration, a Russian drone damaged a private home in Komyshuvaha. Two men, aged 30 and 46, were wounded.

The occupiers dropped a guided aerial bomb on the village of Tavriiske, injuring a 51-year-old man.

All the victims have received the necessary medical care.

Earlier it was reported that two people were also injured in two other villages in the district during the day.
