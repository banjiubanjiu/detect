# War update: 150 combat engagements on front line over past day, Pokrovsk and Kostiantynivka sectors remain most active

According to Ukrinform, the General Staff of the Armed Forces of Ukraine stated this on Facebook regarding the situation as of 08:00 on Tuesday, April 7.

The enemy carried out 84 airstrikes, dropping 242 guided aerial bombs. In addition, the Russians used 8,625 kamikaze drones and conducted 3,227 shelling attacks, including 42 from multiple rocket launcher systems (MRLS).

The Russian army carried out airstrikes in the areas of Orly, Novoselivka, Vidradne, Pysantsi, Lisne, Prosiana in the Dnipropetrovsk region; Vozdvizhivka, Verkhnia Tersa, Huliaipilske, Dolynka, and Svoboda in the Zaporizhzhia region; as well as Mala Slobidka in the Sumy region.

The Ukrainian Defense Forces’ aviation struck six areas where Russian troops and equipment were concentrated.

In the **Northern Slobozhanshchyna** and **Kursk **directions, the enemy carried out five airstrikes using 13 KABs (precision-guided bombs) and conducted 93 shellings, including six from MRLS. Four combat engagements were recorded.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539491-317.jpg)

In the **Southern Slobozhanshchyna** direction, the Russians twice attempted to break through defensive lines near Starytsia and toward Kolodyazne.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539159-267.jpg)

In the **Kupiansk** direction, the enemy attacked eight times near Pishchane and toward Kivsharivka, Novoosynove, Petropavlivka, and Kurylivka.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539180-792.jpg)

In the **Lyman** direction, the invaders attacked six times toward Lyman, Stavky, Novoserhiivka, and Drobysheve.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539203-586.jpg)

In the **Sloviansk **direction, the Defense Forces repelled five enemy attempts to advance toward Rai-Oleksandrivka, Yampil, and Riznykivka.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539225-686.jpg)

In the **Kramatorsk** direction, Russian troops attacked three times in the areas of Pryvillia, Markove, and toward Minkivka.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539246-527.jpg)

In the **Kostiantynivka** direction, the enemy carried out 19 attacks in the areas of Stepanivka, Kleban-Byk, Pleshchiivka, Rusyn Yar, and toward Illinivka, Sofiivka, and Kostiantynivka.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539363-767.jpg)

In the **Pokrovsk** direction, the Defense Forces repelled 31 assault actions by the Russian army in the areas of Bilytske, Rodynske, Myrnohrad, Pokrovsk, Kotlyne, Udachne, Novomykolaivka, Hryshyne, and Muravka.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539384-623.jpg)

In the **Oleksandrivka **direction, the enemy attacked 13 times in the areas of Ivanivka, Myrne, Kalynivske, Pryvillia, Verbove, Zlahoda, and Oleksandrohrad.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539407-399.jpg)

In the **Huliaipole** direction, 11 Russian attacks occurred in the areas of Olenokostiantynivka, Charivne, and toward Sviatopetrivka, Zaliznychne, and Pryluky.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539427-338.jpg)

In the **Orikhiv** direction, the Defense Forces stopped two enemy attempts to advance near Shcherbaky.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539447-167.jpg)

In the **Prydniprovske** direction, the Russian army carried out five assault actions in the areas of the Antonivskyi Bridge and Bilohrudyi Island.

![](https://static.ukrinform.com/photos/2026_04/1775539466-786.jpg)

In the **Volyn** and **Polissya** directions, no signs of Russian offensive groupings have been detected.

As Ukrinform reported, overall combat losses of Russian troops since the start of the full-scale invasion of Ukraine, from February 24, 2022, to April 7, 2026, amount to approximately 1,305,470 personnel.
