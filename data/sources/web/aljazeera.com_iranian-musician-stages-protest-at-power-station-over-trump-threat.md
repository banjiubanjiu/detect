# Iranian musician stages protest at power station over Trump threats

Iranian musician Ali Ghamsari has been performing outside the Damavand power station in a symbolic protest against US threats against civilian infrastructure. The site supplies roughly half of Tehran’s electricity. US President Donald Trump has warned of strikes on Iran’s power plants, bridges and vital facilities.
