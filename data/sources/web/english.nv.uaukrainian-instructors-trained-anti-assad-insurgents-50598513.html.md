# Ukrainian instructors trained anti-Assad insurgents, expert says

April 7, 2026, 08:46 PM

27

[](https://english.nv.ua/nation/ukrainian-instructors-trained-anti-assad-insurgents-50598513.html#)[](https://english.nv.ua/nation/ukrainian-instructors-trained-anti-assad-insurgents-50598513.html#)[](https://english.nv.ua/nation/ukrainian-instructors-trained-anti-assad-insurgents-50598513.html#)[](mailto:news@nv.ua?subject=%D0%9B%D0%B8%D1%81%D1%82%20%D0%B2%20%D1%80%D0%B5%D0%B4%D0%B0%D0%BA%D1%86%D1%96%D1%8E&body=https://english.nv.ua/nation/ukrainian-instructors-trained-anti-assad-insurgents-50598513.html)[](https://english.nv.ua/nation/ukrainian-instructors-trained-anti-assad-insurgents-50598513.html)

![Image 6: Volodymyr Zelenskyy in Damascus, April 5 (Photo: REUTERS/Khalil Ashawi)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/270/766/original/de61a17039a8e39026c32b32a4d674cf.png?q=85&stamp=20260407234514&w=900&f=webp)

Volodymyr Zelenskyy in Damascus, April 5 (Photo: REUTERS/Khalil Ashawi)

Ukrainian instructors helped fighters allied with [Ahmed al‑Sharaa](https://english.nv.ua/nation/engaging-with-syria-can-help-curtail-russian-influence-50598495.html) learn to operate combat drones during the 2024 overthrow of Syrian dictator Bashar al‑Assad, Ukrainian officer and journalist Yuriy Matsarsky told NV Radio on April 7.

“We know that during the toppling of Bashar al‑Assad’s regime in 2024 there were at least Ukrainian specialists — instructors who helped the fighters fighting on the side of al‑Sharaa, who at the time was still known as al-Julani, master drones,” Matsarsky said.

In December 2024, Washington Post wrote that Syrian rebels who toppled Assad received some assistance from Ukraine, including drones and Ukrainian drone operators. The article said that Ukrainian intelligence sent as many as 20 experienced drone operators and about 150 first‑person‑view (FPV) drones to a rebel headquarters in Idlib to assist Hayat Tahrir al‑Sham (HTS), which became a key force in the uprising.
