# Russian demand for personal bunker soars

April 6, 2026, 08:13 PM

![Image 6: Shot/Telegram](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/270/021/original/1adf80629a0b0e962744b6e60d963b92.png?q=85&stamp=20260406230924&w=900&f=webp)

Shot/Telegram

Demand for personal [bunkers](https://english.nv.ua/nation/president-zelesnkyy-shows-his-secret-underground-office-50586486.html) and bomb shelters in Russia has risen 140%, with Moscow’s business elite being the primary customers, Russian propaganda Telegram channel Shot reported on April 6.

The channel said several government officials had also placed orders but did not give names.

 Photo: Shot/Telegram 

The owner of a manufacturing company told Shot that the order backlog for personal shelters now stretches into the summer. Since early March, orders have been arriving almost daily. Requests to install bunkers at cottages and other country properties have increased about 2.5 times.

![Image 9: Shot/Telegram](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/MediaPhoto/images/000/582/797/original/8cc886c9f3ae49174cc555842f6368ab.png?q=85&stamp=20260406230926&f=webp)

 Photo: Shot/Telegram 

Prices start at about RUB2 million ($25,000) for a basic model, a reinforced steel cylinder for two people with minimal comforts, buried up to 5 meters with an air intake. Size and fit outs are customized for an additional fee: buyers can add space, a kitchen, a shower, a sofa and even a TV with a gaming console.

Luxury models cost at least RUB20 million ($250,000) and resemble an underground apartment. Builders first install reinforced concrete walls in the ground, as when constructing subway stations, and then cover the structure with an impact‑resistant slab. The bunkers are fitted with diesel fuel and clean water tanks holding up to 10,000 liters.

Owners can also arrange a secure EMERCOM (Russia’s Ministry of Emergency Situations) radio channel that would transmit an SOS signal from the bunker in case of a major disaster. If outside air is contaminated, the shelter can be sealed and run as an airtight capsule with circulated air, allowing a large family to survive for several months.
