# Russia loses 980 troops, two air defense systems in war against Ukraine over past day

The General Staff of the Ukrainian Armed Forces stated this in a post on Facebook, Ukrinform reports.

Since the beginning of the full-scale war, Ukrainian forces have destroyed 11,841 Russian tanks, 24,364 (+4) armored fighting vehicles, 39,562 (+65) artillery systems, 1,722 (+3) multiple rocket launchers, 1,340 (+2) anti-aircraft warfare systems, and 4,517 cruise missiles. The Russian army has also lost 435 warplanes, 350 helicopters, 223,341 (+1,945) tactical unmanned aerial systems, 33 warships/cutters, 2 submarines, 87,862 (+248) vehicles and fuel trucks, and 4,115 (+3) pieces of special equipment.
