# Russian attacks cause power outages across border communities in Chernihiv region

Ukrinform

In the Chernihiv region, Russian attacks have damaged power facilities in the Novhorod-Siverskyi and Koriukivka districts, leaving settlements in most border communities without power.

This was reported on Facebook by Oleksandr Seliverstov, head of the Novhorod-Siverskyi District Military Administration, according to Ukrinform.

Energy workers will begin emergency repair work as soon as the security situation allows, he added.
