# Oman welcomes ceasefire, praises Pakistan's role for 'lasting solution'

8 April 2026 06:54 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/People%20gather%20after%20a%20two-week%20ceasefire%20in%20the%20Iran%20war%20was%20announced%2C%20in%20Tehran.JPG.jpg?itok=sRPaddf1)

Oman’s foreign ministry welcomed the ceasefire between Iran and the United States and praised Pakistan’s mediation efforts, along with other calls to end the war. The statement underscored the need to build on the truce.

The ministry said efforts should now focus on addressing the root causes of the crisis and achieving a permanent end to hostilities in the region.
