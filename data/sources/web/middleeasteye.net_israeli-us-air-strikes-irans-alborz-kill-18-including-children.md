# Israeli-US air strikes in Iran’s Alborz kill 18, including children

7 April 2026 09:36 BST

![](/sites/default/files/styles/max_2600x2600/public/2026-04/000_A6XH2EW.jpg.jpg?itok=az6vTcg6)

Israeli-US air strikes on residential areas in Iran’s Alborz province have killed at least 18 people, including two children, according to Iranian media reports.

The official Fars news agency reported that the province’s deputy governor confirmed the death toll, adding that 24 others were injured in the attacks.
