# Russian drone kills 3, injures 15 in Nikopol

April 7, 2026, 07:27 AM

![Aftermath of a Russian drone strike (Photo: Oleksandr Hanzha/Telegram)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/270/188/original/b1aa16045976cc57b3a1a7f0ec0f1fa7.png?q=85&stamp=20260407102839&w=900&f=webp)

Aftermath of a Russian drone strike (Photo: Oleksandr Hanzha/Telegram)

At least three people were killed and 15 others were injured in a Russian FPV drone strike on a bus in central Nikopol in Ukraine’s Dnipropetrovsk Oblast early in the morning on April 7, regional Governor Oleksandr Hanza [reported](https://t.me/dnipropetrovskaODA/28606) on Telegram, sharing photos of the aftermath.

The drone hit the bus as it approached a stop, injuring people inside the vehicle and at the stop.

“This was deliberate terror against civilians — against people who were simply going about their business,” Hanza said.

Mykola Lukashuk, head of the Dnipropetrovsk Regional Council, said the victims included two women and one man.
