The Times of Israel is liveblogging Tuesday’s events as they unfold.

#### Iranian missile likely intercepted, IDF says; Hezbollah fires rockets at north

The ballistic missile fired by Iran at southern Israel a short while ago was likely intercepted, according to the IDF.

Meanwhile, sirens sound in the Galilee amid a Hezbollah rocket attack from Lebanon.

#### US oil price jumps over 2% after reported strikes on Iran’s key export terminal Kharg Island

![A motorist fills up the tank of a vehicle at a gasoline station, April 6, 2026, in Greenwood Village, Colorado (AP Photo/David Zalubowski) A motorist fills up the tank of a vehicle at a gasoline station, April 6, 2026, in Greenwood Village, Colorado (AP Photo/David Zalubowski)](https://static-cdn.toi-media.com/www/uploads/2026/04/AP26096814528811-640x400.jpg)

The main US oil contract extends its gains on reports of strikes on the key Iranian oil export terminal of Kharg Island.

West Texas Intermediate for delivery in May jumps 2.1 percent to $114.71 a barrel.

The international benchmark, Brent North Sea crude, for delivery in June, gains 0.4 percent to $110.22 a barrel.

#### Vance says US has ‘tools in our toolkit that we so far haven’t decided to use’ on Iran

![US Vice President JD Vance speaks during a press conference at the former Carmelite Monastery in Budapest, Hungary, April 7, 2026. (Jonathan Ernst/Pool Photo via AP) US Vice President JD Vance speaks during a press conference at the former Carmelite Monastery in Budapest, Hungary, April 7, 2026. (Jonathan Ernst/Pool Photo via AP)](https://static-cdn.toi-media.com/www/uploads/2026/04/AP26097452577749-e1775566880655-640x400.jpg)

US Vice President JD Vance warns the US has “tools in our toolkit that we so far haven’t decided to use” against Iran, adding he was “hopeful” negotiations would avoid them being deployed.

“The United States has largely accomplished its military objectives,” Vance tells reporters during a visit to Hungary, adding that “there’s going to be a lot of negotiation between now and then” when the US deadline expires, at 0000 GMT Wednesday.

“They’ve got to know we’ve got tools in our toolkit that we so far haven’t decided to use. The president of the United States can decide to use them, and he will decide to use them if the Iranians don’t change their course of conduct,” he adds.

#### IDF detects ballistic missile fire from Iran; sirens expected in south

The IDF has identified a new ballistic missile attack from Iran, targeting southern Israel.

Sirens are expected to sound in the coming minutes.

#### Hezbollah rocket fired from Lebanon hits Kiryat Shmona home; no injuries

A Hezbollah rocket fired from Lebanon struck a home in the northern city of Kiryat Shmona, according to rescue services.

No injuries are reported.

#### Israel bombs some 10 ‘key’ rail sections, bridges to stop Iran’s Revolutionary Guard moving weapons

![A railway bridge apparently hit in a strike near Amin Abad, Iran on April 7, 2026 (Screenshot from video posted on X) A railway bridge apparently hit in a strike near Amin Abad, Iran on April 7, 2026 (Screenshot from video posted on X)](https://static-cdn.toi-media.com/www/uploads/2026/04/iran-640x400.jpg)

The Israeli Air Force has bombed around 10 “key” rail sections and bridges in Iran, as part of efforts to prevent the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps from moving weapon systems, security officials tell The Times of Israel.

Ahead of the strikes, the IDF warned Iranians to stay away from trains until this evening.

Security officials say the IRGC had used the rail and highway bridges to move weapons and other military supplies.

Israel has also warned it would strike Iranian “national infrastructure” to cause economic damage to the regime.

#### UK government blocks Kanye West from country after festival backlash over antisemitism

![Ye, the rapper formerly known as Kanye West, leaves federal court during the trial of Sean 'Diddy' Combs in New York, June 13, 2025. (AP/Michael R. Sisak) Ye, the rapper formerly known as Kanye West, leaves federal court during the trial of Sean 'Diddy' Combs in New York, June 13, 2025. (AP/Michael R. Sisak)](https://static-cdn.toi-media.com/www/uploads/2026/01/AP25164588842633-e1769459879674-640x400.jpg)

The British government has blocked US rapper Kanye West from entering Britain on grounds that his presence would not be conducive to the public good, UK media reports.

The update follows mounting criticism over his booking as a headline act at the Wireless music festival in London, set to be held in July.

West, now known as Ye, has made multiple public antisemitic remarks since fall 2022, when he first threatened to go “death con 3 on JEWISH PEOPLE.”

Last year the rapper bought a Super Bowl ad to promote T-shirts with swastikas on them, then released a song titled “Heil Hitler,” to mark the 80th anniversary of the defeat of Nazi Germany in World War II.

He has since expressed regret over his multiple antisemitic rants, which he blamed on his bipolar disorder.

The United Kingdom is seeing soaring antisemitic attacks and incidents, with many in the Jewish community blaming authorities for not taking strong enough action on the matter.

#### Efforts to facilitate talks between US and Iran ongoing, Pakistani sources say

Efforts to facilitate talks between the US and Iran are still ongoing, two Pakistani sources with knowledge of the discussions tells Reuters, as strikes on Iran intensify and a deadline set by US President Donald Trump nears.

#### Man badly hurt after his car explodes in Tira; police probing link to criminal feud

![A wrecked car stands idle on the side of the road after it exploded and burst into flames in Tira, a central Arab city, on April 7, 2026. (Fire and Rescue Service) A wrecked car stands idle on the side of the road after it exploded and burst into flames in Tira, a central Arab city, on April 7, 2026. (Fire and Rescue Service)](https://static-cdn.toi-media.com/www/uploads/2026/04/image-22-e1775565135268-640x400.jpg)

A man was badly injured after his car exploded while driving in the central Arab city of Tira, first responders say.

Firefighters deployed to the scene around noon found the man’s car up in flames on the side of the road. They extinguished the fire and pulled the man, unconscious, from his vehicle.

Magen David Adom paramedics transported the victim to the hospital. His condition is listed as serious.

Police are investigating the incident and link it to a criminal conflict in the city. No suspects have been arrested.

The car exploded near Tira’s city hall, according to local Arab outlets. Many Arab mayors and municipal officials face threats of violence from criminal syndicates.

#### Ahead of deadline, Trump warns ‘a whole civilization will die tonight’ if Iran doesn’t open Hormuz

![US President Donald Trump speaks with reporters during the White House Easter Egg Roll on the South Lawn of the White House, April 6, 2026, in Washington. (AP Photo/Julia Demaree Nikhinson) US President Donald Trump speaks with reporters during the White House Easter Egg Roll on the South Lawn of the White House, April 6, 2026, in Washington. (AP Photo/Julia Demaree Nikhinson)](https://static-cdn.toi-media.com/www/uploads/2026/04/AP26096747664430-640x400.jpg)

Twelve hours before his 8 p.m. Eastern Time ultimatum is set to expire, US President Donald Trump says “a whole civilization will die tonight, never to be brought back again,” if Iran doesn’t agree to his demands for reopening the Strait of Hormuz.

“I don’t want that to happen, but it probably will,” he writes on Truth Social. “However, now that we have Complete and Total Regime Change, where different, smarter, and less radicalized minds prevail, maybe something revolutionarily wonderful can happen, WHO KNOWS?”

Trump says that “we will find out tonight, one of the most important moments in the long and complex history of the World. 47 years of extortion, corruption, and death, will finally end. God Bless the Great People of Iran!”

In an expletive-laden post on Sunday, Trump warned that the US would attack power plants and bridges in Iran starting Tuesday evening if the Islamic Republic failed to reopen the strait.
