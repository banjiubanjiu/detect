# Russian attack sets Pryluky city hall on fire, injuries reported - photos / The New Voice of Ukraine

# Russian drones torch two admin buildings in Chernihiv Oblast

April 7, 2026, 01:52 PM

![Pryluky City Council on fire following Russian attack, April 7 (Photo: Suspilne Chernihiv)](https://static.nv.ua/shared/system/Article/posters/003/270/571/original/b83cbb753bb49dde3d1adfba562128ac.png?q=85&stamp=20260407165303&w=900&f=webp)

Russian forces attacked the center of Pryluky in Ukraine's Chernihiv Oblast, setting the city council building on fire, public broadcaster Suspilne [reported](https://suspilne.media/chernihiv/1283262-gorit-budivla-miskradi-rosijski-vijska-atakuvali-centr-priluk-na-cernigivsini/?utm_source=telegram&utm_medium=ps) on April 7.

Three loud explosions were heard in the city.

According to Regional Governor Vyacheslav Chaus, the enemy targeted administrative buildings in both Pryluky and the nearby city of Novhorod-Siverskyi.

Local authorities are assessing the damage and verifying the number of casualties.

This attack follows a series of strikes on energy infrastructure the previous day, causing widespread power outages across Chernihiv Oblast.
