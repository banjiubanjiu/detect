Taiwan’s Mainland Affairs Council (MAC), the government body responsible for cross-strait policy just exposed the CCP’s real playbook for the KMT’s "peace trip." 

Spokesperson Liang Wen-chieh warns that this meeting is a strategic trap designed to "internalize" the Taiwan issue, rebranding a global security crisis as a private "Chinese family affair."

"Beijing’s objective is to treat Taiwan as a domestic matter... to cut off military procurement from the U.S. and exclude intervention from Japan."

By getting the KMT to play along, Xi Jinping is attempting to build a narrative where the world has no right to help Taiwan. If Taiwan is framed as a "domestic" concern, then U.S. arms sales and Japanese support are suddenly labeled "foreign interference." The KMT isn't opening a dialogue; they are providing the camouflage Beijing needs to isolate Taiwan before a "big bullies small" takeover.
