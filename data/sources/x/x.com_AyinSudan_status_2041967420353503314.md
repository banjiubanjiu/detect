New evidence gathered by Yale University’s Humanitarian Research Lab (HRL) suggests Ethiopia is supporting the Rapid Support Forces (RSF) through an Ethiopian National Defence Force (ENDF) base in Asosa, Ethiopia.

Using satellite imagery and open-source data, the lab reports that the ENDF installation in Asosa Town has been used to support RSF operations across the border into Blue Nile State – including the takeover of Kurmuk in Sudan in late March. 

Analysts fear the involvement of Ethiopia and neighbouring Eritrea could convert Sudan's conflict --now approaching its third anniversary-- into a wider regional conflict.
