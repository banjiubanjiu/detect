In its response to the statement issued by the so‑called “Autonomous Administration” on January 19, 2026; 

📍The Syrian government affirms its rejection of any attempt to instrumentalize terrorism for political or security leverage with the international community. 
📍It states that warnings concerning IS detention facilities constitute a politicized use of the terrorism file aimed at exerting pressure and preserving an authority imposed by force of arms.
📍The Syrian government underscores the readiness of its military and security institutions to continue combating terrorism, to secure all detention centers in line with recognized international standards, and to prevent any escape of I. 
📍It warns the leadership of the SDF against any reckless measures that could facilitate the escape of IS inmates or the opening of prisons as a form of political pressure, describing such actions as war crimes and direct collusion with terrorism.
📍The Syrian government stresses that the restoration of state authority and the rule of law across all Syrian territory is, in its view, the only sustainable guarantee for definitively ending IS, addressing displacement, and rebuilding civil peace on the basis of justice, full national sovereignty, and equal citizenship for all Syrians.
