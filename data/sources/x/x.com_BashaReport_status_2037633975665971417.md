Statement by the Yemeni Armed Forces regarding the declaration of their position on the American-Israeli aggression against Iran and the countries of the axis of jihad and resistance, 27 March 2026

In the name of God, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

God Almighty said, “Indeed, God loves those who fight in His cause in ranks as though they are a solid structure.” God Almighty has spoken the truth.

Proceeding from our religious and moral responsibility, and in light of the brutal aggression carried out by the American and Israeli enemy, which targets the Islamic Republic of Iran, the axis of jihad and resistance, and our entire Islamic nation, with the aim of establishing what is called “Greater Israel,” under the banner of “reshaping the Middle East.”

Within the framework of our nation’s legitimate right to confront the Zionist plan and those who carry it out, namely the United States and Israel, and in affirmation of the importance of cooperation among the peoples and countries of the region in taking a just position to confront the American-Israeli aggression and the Zionist plan, and to work toward inflicting a major defeat upon them.

Based on the principled position of our Yemeni Muslim people in support of our Islamic nation, and in response to any American-Israeli aggression against any Muslim country.

In light of the continued aggression by the American and Israeli enemy against the Islamic Republic of Iran, as well as against Palestine, Gaza, Iraq, and Lebanon, as part of implementing the Zionist plan that threatens the entire الأمة, meaning the whole Islamic nation.

The Yemeni Armed Forces, out of concern for regional security and for stopping the aggression, affirm the following.

First: The immediate need for the American and Israeli enemy to respond to international diplomatic efforts to stop the aggression against Iran and the countries of the axis, as this is an unjust, oppressive, and unjustified aggression that harms global and regional stability and security, and also damages the global economy.

Second: The immediate need to halt the aggression against Muslim countries in Palestine, Lebanon, Iran, and Iraq, and to lift the unjust blockade imposed on Yemen.

Third: The need to implement the Gaza agreement and to fulfill the commitments it includes, both humanitarian obligations and the legitimate rights of the Palestinian people.

Fourth: We affirm that we are ready for direct military intervention in any of the following cases:

I) If any additional alliances join with the United States and Israel against the Islamic Republic of Iran and the axis of jihad and resistance.

II) If the Red Sea is used to carry out hostile operations by the United States and Israel against the Islamic Republic of Iran or against any Muslim country. We will not allow this.

III) If escalation continues against the Islamic Republic and the axis of jihad and resistance, in accordance with what the military situation requires.

Fifth: The Yemeni Armed Forces warn against any unjust measures aimed at tightening the blockade on the Yemeni people.

In conclusion

We affirm that our military operations are directed at the Israeli and American enemy in order to defeat the Zionist plan, and they do not target any Muslim people.

God is sufficient for us, and He is the best disposer of affairs, the best protector and the best supporter.

Long live Yemen, free, proud, and independent.

Victory to Yemen and to all free people of the nation.

Sanaa, 8 Shawwal 1447 AH
