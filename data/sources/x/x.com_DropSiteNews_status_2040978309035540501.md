💢 Abu Obeida, spokesperson for the Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades, rejected proposals for Hamas’ armed wing to disarm on Sunday, calling them a “blatant attempt” by Israel to continue killing and achieve the extermination of Palestinians.

In a recorded statement, he said such demands would not be accepted “under any circumstances,” describing them as an effort to compensate for Israel’s failures on the battlefield.

He emphasized that armed resistance remains the primary means of defense, adding that what Israel failed to achieve “by tanks and annihilation” it will not achieve through political pressure or negotiations.

He also described the war as ongoing and the movement’s response as strategic, and not reactive.
